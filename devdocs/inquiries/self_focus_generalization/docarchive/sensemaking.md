### Key Insights

- **KI1 (genus/species distinction)** — Exploration's counterexamples force a genus/species recognition: Self-Focus is a species; the genus is non-extractive attention (attention that is not oriented toward harvesting approval/validation from the current counterparty). Six species identified: Self-Focus, Mission-Focus, Caregiver-Focus, Task-Focus, Dialogue-Focus, Transcendent-Focus. All produce the same observer experience and the same multiplicative gating effect.
- **KI2 (observer-invariance)** — The modulator effect on attachment depends on what the OBSERVER reads — the displayed signals. Internal state matters only insofar as it produces consistent displayed signals over time. Short-term, the display is decoupled from the internal content (narcissist parasocial dynamics; performer on stage; persona effects).
- **KI3 (moment-vs-meta timescale)** — Narcissistic attention at the MOMENT-TO-MOMENT interaction level IS self-focused (attention on own greatness/story/image). At the META level (orienting life around validation) it's approval-harvesting. APT's modulator operates at moment-to-moment level. So narcissists genuinely fit the Self-Focus axis at the interaction level; their pathology shows over longer timescales. This reconciles Claim N cleanly.
- **KI4 (prescriptive vs descriptive confusion)** — Iteration-2's pro-social reframe ("Self-Focus is narcissism's opposite") conflated two levels. DESCRIPTIVELY, the mechanism produces the same attachment for both healthy and pathological self-focus. PRESCRIPTIVELY, the user should cultivate healthy Self-Focus (non-exploitative, sustainable) rather than pathological self-absorption (exploitative, unsustainable). Iteration-3 should separate these.
- **KI5 (adjacent mechanisms are external)** — Intermittent reinforcement, grandiosity signaling, scarcity, trauma bonding, social proof, progressive commitment, isolation — these are REAL attachment-generating mechanisms that explain exaggerated devotion (cults, narcissism, celebrity) but are NOT part of APT's Self-Positioning. APT should reference them as "adjacent theories" without absorbing them. This is honest scoping.
- **KI6 (evolutionary primitivity)** — The mechanism operates cross-species (animal training) and cross-scale (market-level branding). Suggests an evolutionary-primitive pattern rather than a culturally-specific one. This strengthens theoretical weight — the theory is describing a fundamental psychological pattern, not a local cultural artifact.
- **KI7 (the asymmetric-devotion case)** — The user's examples (narcissist-followers; dating paradox) are specifically about *asymmetric* devotion (one worshipper, one worshipped) rather than *mutual* attachment. APT's directional/asymmetric measurement already handles this (iteration-1 Cluster 2), but the finding could make the asymmetric-devotion pattern explicit.

## Phase 3 — Ambiguity Collapse

### Ambiguity 1 — Should the axis be renamed to Non-Extractive Attention, or should Self-Focus be kept with subtypes recognized?

**Resolution: Rename the axis to Non-Extractive Attention at the genus level; retain Self-Focus as the named primary species.**

**Reasoning:**
- The genus is what the mechanism actually tracks. Naming at species-level is structurally inaccurate (forces the theory to treat Self-Focus as primary when Caregiver-Focus, Mission-Focus etc. operate by the same mechanism).
- Renaming preserves predictive unity — one axis, not six exceptions.
- Continuity: Self-Focus doesn't disappear; it becomes explicitly the species most relevant to value-establishing contexts. Users familiar with Self-Focus can continue using it for their primary application.

**Counter-interpretation test:** Could we keep Self-Focus as primary and document the other cases as "edge cases where the mechanism surprisingly generalizes"? This is the status-quo preservation option. It fails because:
- It violates C2 (patching-style accommodation)
- The counterexamples aren't "edge cases" — they're as common as the primary case (mothers, teachers, nurses, saints are ubiquitous)
- Theoretical economy is sacrificed for historical naming

**Structural reason the counter fails:** The failure is *completeness* (the Self-Focus-as-primary framing systematically excludes half the phenomena); the fix is *generalization* (genus-level axis). Not a naming preference — a structural requirement.

**What's now fixed:** The iteration-3 axis name is Non-Extractive Attention. Self-Focus is a species.
**What's no longer allowed:** Using "Self-Focus" as the axis label in iteration-3 documentation. Self-Focus is a species name.
**What depends on this:** finding.md section structure, 2×2 axis label, 5-signal catalog framing, user-facing diagnostic wording.
**What changed in the model:** The axis is now genus-level. Individual users and phenomena can be characterized by which species of non-extractive attention they primarily operate in.

### Ambiguity 2 — Which species get first-class status in iteration-3 documentation?

**Resolution: Three primary species (Self-Focus, Mission-Focus, Caregiver-Focus) with first-class treatment; three secondary species (Task-Focus, Dialogue-Focus, Transcendent-Focus) as refinements noted.**

**Reasoning:**
- Self-Focus: the user's primary application (LinkedIn, dating, sales, value-establishing contexts). First-class.
- Mission-Focus: covers a large class of phenomena (political leaders, activists, organizational visionaries). First-class because distinct from Self-Focus in producing different flavors of attachment (follower vs peer).
- Caregiver-Focus: covers another large class (mothers, therapists, nurses, teachers). First-class because its attention-direction is externally on the counterparty yet mechanism is non-extractive. This is the most theoretically important distinction to highlight.
- Task-Focus: largely overlaps with Self-Focus (attention on the task vs attention on own priorities — nearly the same thing). Secondary — note as an optional framing for performance-psychology applications.
- Dialogue-Focus: a sub-variant of Caregiver-Focus or Mission-Focus (attention on the mutual inquiry). Rare in isolated form. Secondary.
- Transcendent-Focus: attention on abstract ideal (god, truth). Rare in professional contexts. Secondary; noted for saints/contemplatives.

**Counter-interpretation test:** Could we include all six as first-class? Concern: over-specification, spec bloat, diminishing returns. Three covers the major distinct mechanisms observers actually encounter.

**What changed in the model:** Species-level specificity is introduced at the right granularity.

### Ambiguity 3 — How is narcissism reconciled with the theory?

**Resolution: Narcissism is self-focused at the moment-to-moment interaction level (fits the Self-Focus species) and produces exaggerated attachment via ADJACENT mechanisms (intermittent reinforcement, grandiosity signaling, social-norm violation, love-bombing, trauma bonding) that are outside APT's core scope.**

**Reasoning:**
- At the moment-to-moment interaction level (which is where the modulator operates), narcissists ARE self-focused — their attention is on their own greatness, story, image. They fit the Self-Focus species mechanically.
- The pathology is at the META level (their life is oriented around validation-harvesting) and at the timescale level (they exploit the attachment they generate). APT's modulator doesn't measure at these timescales.
- Exaggerated attachment requires ADDITIVE mechanisms beyond pure Self-Focus. APT's spec references these as "adjacent mechanisms outside scope."

**Counter-interpretation test:** Could we claim narcissists are actually non-self-focused (extractive, attending to validation)? This is the iteration-2 pro-social-reframe stance. Fails because:
- The observer reads self-focused behavior in narcissistic interactions (they dominate airtime, they talk about themselves, they show selective engagement — all self-focused signals)
- If narcissists were extractively other-focused at the interaction level, they'd display Supplication signals (self-justification, hedging, over-explaining) — and they don't (often the opposite)
- The observer's read produces the attachment effect; the observer reads Self-Focus

**Structural reason the counter fails:** The counter confuses META-level pathology (approval-seeking across life) with INTERACTION-level attention. They're separate timescales. APT operates at the interaction level; narcissism's interaction-level pattern is self-focused with exploitative consequences downstream.

**What's now fixed:** Narcissism is self-focused at the moment-to-moment level (fits Self-Focus species). Exaggerated devotion requires adjacent mechanisms beyond APT's scope.
**What's no longer allowed:** Claiming narcissism doesn't fit the mechanism (iteration-2 pro-social reframe claim).
**What depends on this:** The iteration-3 finding's explanation of Claim N; the pro-social reframe refinement.
**What changed in the model:** Narcissism is no longer an exception but a case covered by the Self-Focus species + external adjacent mechanisms. Makes the theory more honest.

## Phase 5 — Conceptual Stabilization

### Final Conceptual Model

The Self-Focus theory from iteration-2, when tested against a wide range of attachment-generating phenomena, reveals itself to be one species of a broader genus: **Non-Extractive Attention**. The mechanism — attention not oriented toward harvesting approval from the current counterparty — operates identically whether that non-extractive attention is on own priorities (Self-Focus), on a larger cause (Mission-Focus), on the counterparty's wellbeing without approval-seeking (Caregiver-Focus), on the craft or task (Task-Focus), on a shared inquiry (Dialogue-Focus), or on a transcendent ideal (Transcendent-Focus). All six species produce the same modulator effect on attachment via the multiplicative gating: `Attachment ≈ f(charm, hope, fear) × g(non-extractive attention)`.

The negative pole — Extractive Attention or Supplication — is approval-harvesting. Its failure signature is the devaluation pattern established in iteration-1: high charm/hope/fear signals discounted post-hoc because the other party perceives asymmetric-need.

Narcissism is NOT an exception to the theory. At the moment-to-moment interaction level (where the modulator operates), narcissistic behavior IS self-focused — attention on own story, greatness, image. Narcissism fits the Self-Focus species. Exaggerated devotion to narcissists requires additional mechanisms (intermittent reinforcement, grandiosity signaling, trauma bonding, etc.) that are outside APT's modulator mechanism but real.

Iteration-2's pro-social reframe ("Self-Focus is narcissism's opposite") was descriptively incorrect. At the mechanism level, calibrated Self-Focus and narcissistic self-absorption produce similar attachment signals for observers. They diverge at sustainability and ethical consequence (healthy vs exploitative) — but APT describes the mechanism, not the ethical consequence. Prescriptive guidance (cultivate sustainable healthy non-extractive attention) is a separate layer.

The architectural spine of iteration-1 (Path α, multiplicative gating, trait-with-bearings, 5-signal catalog, 2×2 diagnostic, devaluation signature) is **preserved without change**. Only the vocabulary at the axis level refines.

For the user's concrete LinkedIn diagnostic: Self-Focus remains the operationally relevant species; the 5-question audit and 2×2 typology still work; the species label indicates this is one flavor of non-extractive attention (the one most relevant to value-establishing contexts).
