# Finding: self_focus_generalization

**This finding is iteration-3 of the APT Self-Positioning theory.** It supersedes `apt_self_focus_reframe/finding.md` at the vocabulary / species-taxonomy / pro-social-refinement / Cluster 4-status level. It inherits the architectural commitments from `apt_missing_dimension/finding.md` (iteration-1) unchanged: Path α (named modulator between Domain 1 and Domain 2), multiplicative gating mechanism, trait-with-bearings structure, devaluation failure signature, 5-signal catalog, 2×2 diagnostic structure.

What changes in iteration-3: the axis is renamed at the theoretical level (Self-Focus → Non-Extractive Attention, as genus); the species list is introduced (two primary species with four secondary); the narcissism reconciliation gains a temporal arc with signaling-theory mimicry framing; the pro-social reframe's descriptive claim is retracted and re-positioned as prescriptive guidance; an adjacent-mechanisms section is added with ecological scoping language; theoretical grounding (ethology, Rogers' Unconditional Positive Regard, performance-psychology task-focus, signaling theory) is integrated; a two-level naming convention preserves the user's LinkedIn vocabulary; and Cluster 4 (APT-level substrate doubt) is elevated from "flagged with reopening conditions" to "iteration-4 substrate reframe inquiry should be planned for future."

## Question

The prior inquiry (`apt_self_focus_reframe`) established **Self-Focus** as the causal-directional axis of APT's Self-Positioning modulator and gave the user a working LinkedIn diagnostic. The user then proposed two specific tests of that theory's generalization:

- **Claim N (Narcissist-followers):** Narcissistic individuals reliably accumulate devoted followers / worshippers, while smart / helpful / attractive non-self-focused individuals often don't receive the same commitment.
- **Claim D (Dating paradox):** A man who is visibly self-focused and doesn't chase can generate strong attraction and compliance from women, even without material advantages (wealth, looks). The same man, becoming attentive / giving / pursuing, triggers dismissal.

The user then asked: are there other examples that support the Self-Focus finding?

**The inquiry question:** Does the Self-Focus / Self-Positioning theory generalize beyond LinkedIn / value-establishing contexts to explain the narcissist-follower dynamic, the dating paradox, and other interpersonal attachment phenomena — and if so, which domains does it cover, what boundary conditions emerge, and does it surface theoretical refinements that warrant an iteration-3 update?

**The goal:** A structured deliverable with three parts: (1) a catalog of phenomena the theory explains with mechanism tracing; (2) honest boundary conditions where it fails; (3) any theoretical refinements the wider application forces.

One important tension flagged at branch-time: iteration-2's pro-social reframe claimed "Self-Focus is narcissism's opposite." Claim N implies narcissists produce the same attachment the theory predicts for Self-Focused individuals. The inquiry committed to resolving this explicitly rather than dodging it.

## Finding

### The generalization is confirmed with a theoretical refinement

The theory generalizes widely, but the generalization forces a **genus/species refactor** at the axis level. Self-Focus turns out to be one species of a broader genus — **Non-Extractive Attention** — and the counterexamples (mothers, caring teachers, Gandhi, Mother Teresa, crisis workers, saints) are other species of the same genus. All species produce the same observer experience ("this person's attention is not on extracting my approval") and the same multiplicative gating effect on attachment.

### The axis: Non-Extractive Attention (two-level naming)

**At the theoretical level:** **Non-Extractive Attention** is the axis. The name describes what the mechanism actually tracks — attention that is not oriented toward harvesting approval / validation / response from the current counterparty. The negative pole is **Supplication** (attention explicitly oriented toward securing the other's response — approval-harvesting).

**At the user-facing level (for your LinkedIn diagnostic):** **Self-Focus** is the species name you continue to use. Your 5-question audit, 2×2 typology, and diagnostic workflow from iteration-2 remain functional without changes. Self-Focus is the species most relevant to value-establishing contexts (professional outreach, networking, first-contact dating, sales).

This two-level naming follows a standard pattern in technical specs (physics: *force* at theory level, *push* at casual level; medicine: *myocardial infarction* at theory level, *heart attack* at casual level; systems: *latency* at theory level, *delay* at casual level). It preserves user vocabulary while keeping the theoretical label honest about what the mechanism actually tracks.

### The species

**Primary species (first-class in the theory):**

- **Self-Focus** — attention on one's own priorities / agenda. The species most relevant to value-establishing contexts. This is the species your LinkedIn application uses. External correlates: scarce investment, unrushed rhythm, calibrated terseness, exit-willingness, low self-justification density.

- **Caregiver-Focus** — non-extractive attention on the counterparty's wellbeing. The caregiver attends fully to the other person, but *not for approval-extraction* — they're attending in order to care, not to harvest reaction. Exemplars: mothers, therapists, devoted teachers, nurses, good-listener friends. Operationalized cleanly by Carl Rogers' Unconditional Positive Regard (UPR) in therapeutic research (congruence + unconditional positive regard + empathic understanding).

**Why these two are primary (not three):** Self-Focus and Caregiver-Focus pass the orthogonality test cleanly — a person can be Self-Focused without being Caregiver-Focused and vice versa, with distinct observable correlates. Mission-Focus (for cause-driven figures like Gandhi) was initially a candidate for primary status in earlier drafts, but its boundary with Self-Focus weakens under inspection: a person deeply attending to their chosen mission IS attending to their own chosen priority, so Mission-Focus often collapses into Self-Focus where the priority happens to be a larger cause. This weaker orthogonality demoted Mission-Focus to secondary.

**Secondary species (noted as refinements, applicable in specific contexts):**

- **Mission-Focus** — attention on a larger cause / purpose. Exemplars: Gandhi, Mother Teresa, visionary founders, revolutionary leaders. When the chosen cause is larger than the self, the reliability signal to observers is similar to Self-Focus but with a distinct texture (follower-generating rather than peer-generating).

- **Task-Focus** — attention on the craft / the work itself. From performance psychology — athletes, musicians, expert practitioners. Task-focus has a rich pedagogy (pre-performance routines, process cues, body-scan grounding, outcome-focus vs task-focus distinction) that is directly importable as **Self-Focus cultivation tools**.

- **Dialogue-Focus** — attention on a shared inquiry / mutual co-thinking. Exemplars: Socratic dialogue, expert peer conversations. The attention is genuinely with the counterparty but non-extractive because it's on the shared object, not on extracting their reaction to you.

- **Transcendent-Focus** — attention on an abstract ideal / god / truth. Exemplars: saints, contemplative practitioners. Rare in professional contexts; relevant for understanding religious devotion and certain moral-exemplar cases.

### Mechanism (unchanged from iteration-1)

The multiplicative gating mechanism holds unchanged:

```
Attachment ≈ f(charm, hope, fear) × g(non-extractive attention)
```

Any species of non-extractive attention produces `g(high)`, amplifying the attachment variables. Supplication produces `g(low)`, collapsing them. The failure signature — devaluation ("this guy doesn't deserve this" despite high charm / hope / fear) — is unchanged.

Each attachment variable is individually modulated:
- **Charm** under low Self-Focus reads as shiny-but-hollow → devalued
- **Hope** under low Self-Focus reads as desperate-to-please → offered benefit loses perceived value
- **Fear** under low Self-Focus reads as bluster → fails to consolidate into respect

### Narcissism reconciliation (with temporal arc)

**The core reconciliation:** Narcissism does NOT contradict the theory. At the moment-to-moment interaction level — which is where the modulator operates — narcissistic attention IS self-focused. The narcissist's attention is on their own greatness, story, image. They fit the Self-Focus species mechanically. The observer reads Self-Focus signals (talking about self, selective engagement, not optimizing for approval in the moment) and generates attachment accordingly.

**What makes narcissist-follower devotion *exaggerated*:** Additional mechanisms layer on top of the Self-Focus signal. Intermittent reinforcement (narcissist's warm/cold cycles create variable-ratio reward schedules), grandiosity signaling (high-status cue), social-norm violation (signals independence from ordinary constraints), love-bombing (high-intensity early engagement creates investment-asymmetry), and trauma bonding (abuse-reward neurochemistry) all amplify attachment beyond what pure Self-Focus would produce. These are adjacent mechanisms, outside APT's modulator scope but real and well-documented.

**Why narcissist-follower relationships eventually burn out (the temporal arc):** This is where iteration-3 adds a genuinely new explanatory layer, via **signaling theory with mimicry-arms-race framing**.

Non-Extractive Attention is a costly signal — genuinely non-extractive behavior requires foregoing optimization for the current exchange (you lose the opportunity to maximize your short-term extraction). Observers read the visible cost and infer reliability ("this person isn't performing for my response, so their behavior is informative, not strategic"). Over evolutionary time, this signal became reliable because mimicry was expensive.

Narcissists run a **mimicry strategy**. They display Non-Extractive Attention (selective engagement, disregard of ordinary reciprocity, talking-about-self) without actually paying the full cost — internally, their attention is on meta-level approval harvesting, not on their own priorities per se. In the short term, the display produces similar attachment to genuine Non-Extractive Attention. Over longer timescales, mimicry is detected (the cost isn't actually being paid consistently; inconsistencies surface; the initial display was intensity-mismatched with sustained behavior). Followers develop counter-signals, attachment decays, and relationships enter cycles of devaluation-and-reconciliation before eventual escape.

**Implication for the pro-social framing:** At the immediate mechanism level, genuine Non-Extractive Attention and narcissistic mimicry produce similar attachment (this is why iteration-2's "Self-Focus is narcissism's opposite" descriptive claim was wrong). At the temporal level, they diverge — genuine attention produces sustainable attachment; mimicry produces attachment-decay and burnout. The prescriptive guidance "cultivate genuine non-extractive attention, not mimicry" is honest because sustainability differs, not because the mechanism discriminates at first contact.

### Dating paradox (Claim D) — clean confirmation

The dating paradox is a clean Self-Focus species case, directly explained by directional/asymmetric modulation:

- Man-A (self-focused, doesn't chase): `Attachment(A→B) ≈ f(charm, hope, fear) × g(high Self-Focus)` → amplified. Woman registers "he has his own thing; he's not trying to extract my approval" and attaches.
- Same man-B (attentive, giving, pursuing): `Attachment(B→A) ≈ f(charm, hope, fear) × g(low Self-Focus)` → collapses. Woman registers "he needs my response more than I need his" and discounts even the high-value signals.

The asymmetric-devotion case (one worshipper, one worshipped) is predicted directly by the trait-with-bearings structure's directional measurement. Adjacent mechanisms (commitment-market-value signaling, intermittent reinforcement, scarcity) co-act but are not required — the Self-Focus mechanism alone predicts the asymmetry.

### Counterexamples (mothers, Gandhi, Socrates, saints, crisis workers) — absorbed via species

All counterexamples are absorbed cleanly via species other than Self-Focus:

- **Mothers, devoted therapists, nurses, hospice workers** → **Caregiver-Focus** (operationalized via Rogers' UPR: congruence + unconditional positive regard + empathic understanding)
- **Gandhi, Mother Teresa, Nelson Mandela, cause-driven activists** → **Mission-Focus**
- **Socrates, philosophical mentors, research mentors** → **Dialogue-Focus**
- **Saints, contemplative practitioners** → **Transcendent-Focus**
- **Expert athletes, musicians in flow** → **Task-Focus**
- **Beloved teachers** → typically **Caregiver-Focus** with **Dialogue-Focus** overlap

Each of these produces intense attachment via the same multiplicative gating mechanism. The attention is genuinely non-extractive (not oriented toward harvesting approval from the current counterparty). The species-level label describes what the attention IS on, not whether it's non-extractive — non-extractivity is the genus property, and all species share it.

### Pro-social refinement

**Iteration-2's descriptive claim — "Self-Focus here is narcissism's opposite" — is explicitly retracted.** At the mechanism level, the theory is indifferent to whether the non-extractive attention is healthy or pathological-mimicked. Both produce similar immediate attachment in observers. The "opposite" framing was descriptively wrong.

**The prescriptive guidance is kept but repositioned as a separate layer:** "Cultivate **genuine** non-extractive attention. Mimicked displays (narcissism and its variants) produce similar immediate attachment but attachment-decay over time as the cost of genuine non-extractive attention isn't actually being paid. Species-fit-to-context matters — Self-Focus is appropriate for value-establishing contexts (professional outreach, dating, networking); Caregiver-Focus is appropriate for caring relationships; Mission-Focus is appropriate when leading a cause."

This is honest scoping: the mechanism doesn't discriminate, but the sustainability of attachment-generation does. Cultivating genuine non-extractive attention is the path to sustained healthy attachment; cultivating mimicry is the path to exploitative short-term attachment that burns out.

### Adjacent mechanisms (outside APT's scope but real)

The theory's scope is the Self-Positioning modulator mechanism. Several real attachment-generating mechanisms are adjacent but not part of APT's modulator. They contribute to extreme-devotion cases (cults, narcissist-follower pathology, celebrity worship, charismatic political movements). Iteration-3 explicitly names them so readers aren't confused about what APT does and doesn't explain:

1. **Intermittent reinforcement** — variable-ratio reward schedules generate stronger attachment than predictable reward (behavioral psychology; slot-machine research).
2. **Grandiosity signaling** — high-status / competence display (dark-triad literature).
3. **Social-norm violation** — "I'm not bound by what binds ordinary people" signals high status or independent power.
4. **Scarcity of access** — limited availability raises perceived value.
5. **Progressive commitment mechanisms** — small yes → large yes (foot-in-door).
6. **Trauma bonding** — abuse-reward cycles create neurochemical attachment.
7. **Social proof** — existing devotees evidence target's value.
8. **Isolation** — removing alternative attachment figures.
9. **Aspirational distance** — gap between self and target creates desire.

**Scoping statement (ecological framing):** These are *signal-exploitation strategies evolutionarily adjacent to genuine non-extractive attention — parasitic or predatory at the attention-economy level*. They co-occur with non-extractive attention signals (particularly in pathological cases) but operate by different mechanisms. Future APT extension inquiries may formalize some of these as additional modulators if warranted by evidence.

### Theoretical grounding

Iteration-3 integrates four external grounding sources, each serving specific explanatory work:

1. **Ethology / evolutionary psychology** (one-paragraph grounding): Social mammals select for reliable-responsive-to-reality individuals over politically-optimized ones. APT's modulator is the observer's evolutionarily-shaped heuristic for detecting this reliability. Cross-species evidence (animal training, pack dynamics) confirms the mechanism operates at a level more primitive than culturally-constructed status.

2. **Carl Rogers' Unconditional Positive Regard** (Caregiver-Focus operationalization): Rogers' three conditions (congruence, unconditional positive regard, empathic understanding) are the operationalization of Caregiver-Focus. The spec imports Rogers' decades of refinement on how the attitude is cultivated, what threatens it (counter-transference, burnout, over-identification), and what sustains it (genuine congruence with one's values).

3. **Performance-psychology task-focus** (Self-Focus cultivation reference): Athletes, musicians, and performers distinguish task-focus (attention on the action/craft) from outcome-focus (attention on the audience's response). Elite performers train into task-focus. APT can directly import pre-performance routines, process cues, body-scan grounding, and outcome-focus-vs-task-focus protocols as tools for cultivating Self-Focus in value-establishing contexts.

4. **Signaling theory / mimicry arms race** (narcissism temporal arc): Non-extractive attention is a costly signal — observer-reliable because mimicry is expensive. Narcissism is a mimicry strategy that succeeds short-term but is detected over time via inconsistencies between display and sustained behavior. This explains the characteristic arc of narcissist-follower relationships (intense initial attachment → gradual devaluation → escape).

## Reasoning

### Why ALPHA (full genus/species refactor) over alternatives

**BETA (moderate refactor — keep Self-Focus as axis with broadened definition) was KILLED on D5 (Definitional Distinctness).** The proposed broadening of "Self-Focus" to include attention on own-priorities-that-center-on-others, own-chosen-roles, own-transcendent-orientations turns the label into a synonym for "any genuinely-held attention direction." A label that names "attention on self OR attention on others that you chose to care about OR attention on ideals you chose to serve" is no longer specifically *self*-focus — it's non-extractive attention with a misleading name. The label-definition mismatch is structurally incoherent. The D4 benefit (preserve user LinkedIn vocabulary) that motivated BETA is achieved more honestly by ALPHA's two-level naming (theoretical Non-Extractive Attention + user-facing Self-Focus), which gets the same D4 outcome without the D5 failure.

**GAMMA (minimalist — iteration-2 addendum with "related cases" section) was KILLED on D1 (Generalization Adequacy) + D7 (Cluster 4 Honesty).** The inquiry's whole point was to determine whether the theory explains phenomena beyond LinkedIn. A minimalist iteration-3 that documents counterexamples as "related cases" without theoretical integration doesn't answer the inquiry — it defers the answer. Gandhi, Mother Teresa, caring mothers, Socrates-style teachers are not *edge cases*; they're central attachment-generating phenomena as common as the LinkedIn case. Calling them "related" without mechanism-tracing is the patching-style accommodation that sensemaking's C2 constraint explicitly warned against. Additionally, GAMMA would keep Cluster 4 status unchanged despite three-mechanism + cross-discipline convergence — this is the "comfortable dismissal" pattern that survival-bias warnings anticipate. GAMMA's virtues (D4, D6, D2) are real but insufficient to compensate for D1 + D7 failures.

**DELTA (Task-Focus as theoretical primary instead of Self-Focus) was KILLED on D4 (User Actionability) + D5 (Definitional Distinctness).** Three-level naming (Non-Extractive Attention theoretical / Task-Focus primary / Self-Focus user-facing) is adoption-hostile — the user just internalized Self-Focus vocabulary in iteration-2, and putting another level in between increases cognitive load for their LinkedIn application. Additionally, if Task-Focus is the primary and Self-Focus is a sub-case (where the task is self-directed agenda), what happens to Caregiver-Focus and Mission-Focus? Either they also collapse into Task-Focus sub-cases (which erases the Caregiver vs Self orthogonality) or they remain sibling primaries (creating a weird structural asymmetry with one primary-that-has-sub-cases and others that don't). The performance-psychology pedagogy that motivated promoting Task-Focus is preserved as a grounding reference for Self-Focus cultivation without requiring the naming promotion — pedagogy can be imported without elevating the label.

**ALPHA SURVIVED on all critical dimensions** with one refinement surfaced by critique: Mission-Focus demoted from primary to secondary species. The orthogonality test between Self-Focus and Mission-Focus is weaker than between Self-Focus and Caregiver-Focus. A person attending to "their mission" is typically attending to "their chosen priority" — Mission-Focus collapses partially into Self-Focus where the priority is a larger cause. Caregiver-Focus doesn't collapse: someone attending to a patient's wellbeing non-extractively is doing something distinctly different from attending to their own goals. The final primary set is therefore two: Self-Focus + Caregiver-Focus. Mission-Focus, Task-Focus, Dialogue-Focus, Transcendent-Focus are secondary species — real and named but without first-class spec treatment in iteration-3.
