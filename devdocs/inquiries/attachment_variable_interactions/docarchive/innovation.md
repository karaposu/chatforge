## Mechanism Application

### Lens Shifting

- **P2 (Specificity):** Under PRAGMA operational conditions, specificity is the most directly detectable dimension — template-match rate, rare-content markers, vocabulary match. Lens: from theoretical-clarity to machine-detection-ready.
- **P4 (TYPE):** Under strategic or clinical use, TYPE matters more than MAGNITUDE. A coerced attachment at high magnitude is worse than a bonded attachment at low magnitude. Lens: academic-taxonomy → consequential-diagnostic.
- **P5 (Worked example):** Reader is likely also a sender of messages. The example is pedagogically more powerful if oriented prescriptively ("what would Person B need to change?"). Lens: descriptive → actionable.

### Combination

- **P2 + P3 (Specificity + Sender-SP):** Generic messages fail on BOTH layers simultaneously. Low specificity suppresses effective_magnitude at the f-layer. Supplication-style message collapses sender-g at the g-layer. Both mechanisms fire at once — this "double-collapse" is a named insight more powerful than either alone.
- **P1 + P4 (Additive f + TYPE):** Additive structure is what makes TYPE directly readable. Because f = Σ(weighted variable contributions), TYPE emerges directly from which variable dominates the sum — no separate TYPE-extraction mechanism needed. Additive architecture gifts TYPE legibility for free.
- **P3-C + P5-C (Display IS state + Authenticity-as-structural-requirement):** If the message-style display IS the Self-Positioning state (not evidence of it), then no optimization path exists for generic templates. Any attempt to "seem specific" without genuine engagement produces detectable incongruence.

### Inversion

- **P1 (Invert: not defensive parsimony, but positive structural claim):** Additivity isn't "assumed until proven otherwise" — it's the correct structural description of how independent signal channels are integrated. Charm, Hope, Fear, Resonance are drawn from distinct evidence sources. Independent channels sum; they don't gate each other.
- **P4 (Invert: TYPE primary, MAGNITUDE secondary):** Current intuition: MAGNITUDE is the primary question, TYPE is the secondary label. Inversion: TYPE predicts behavioral trajectory; MAGNITUDE predicts current intensity. Trajectory beats intensity in strategic, relational, and therapeutic contexts.
- **P5 (Invert: start from failure, derive prescription):** Instead of showing Person A succeeds → Person B fails, invert: show what Person B would need to change. The prescriptive direction is more useful and reveals a structural insight about authenticity.
- **P3 (Invert: display-reveals-state → display-IS-state):** Message style doesn't just allow inference of SP state. In interpersonal contexts, how you write IS your Self-Focus. Display = state.

### Constraint Manipulation

- **Add PRAGMA operationalization constraint:** Every definition must be detectable by a signal-reading system, not just a human. Forces P2 (specificity) to have measurable indicators.
- **Add "text-only channel" constraint to P3:** In Reddit/LinkedIn contexts, there's no voice, no body language. Only message style carries the sender-SP signal. This tightens the mechanism and removes the need to caveat multimodal cases.
- **Remove "per-variable specificity" constraint:** What if specificity is a single global property of the message? Probing this shows per-variable specificity is more accurate (a message can be specific about Resonance content but generic about Hope offer). Removing and re-adding the constraint justifies the per-variable model.

### Absence Recognition

- **Missing: formal definition of "generic."** A signal is generic if the same content can be sent, with trivial modifications, to N > 1 recipients. Specificity = inverse of genericness. This is operationalizable.
- **Missing: the double-collapse causal sequence.** We know generic messages fail, but no document traces the sequence: low specificity → low effective_magnitude → low f-sum; AND Supplication-style → low sender-g₁ → low g-product. Both at once.
- **Missing: illustration of high-TYPE at low-MAGNITUDE.** Theory implies this exists (low-intensity but bonded-qualitative attachment). No example provided. Worth a note in P4.
- **Missing: what changes if specificity is threshold, not linear.** The branch seeded this; P2-C addresses it.

### Domain Transfer

- **Information theory:** Specificity maps to information content. A generic message carries near-zero information about the recipient's specific attributes — it could have been generated without reading anything. A specific message is high-information: it could only be generated by actual engagement. High-information signals have higher effective magnitude.
- **Costly-signal theory (evolutionary psychology):** Specific knowledge about a person is costly to acquire. Displaying it in a message signals genuine investment. Generic templates are cheap signals; receivers discount them. This grounds specificity in adaptive cognition.
- **Sales training:** "A pitch that could be given to anyone tells the buyer you don't understand their problem." Well-documented failure mode in complex sales. Direct analog to Person B.
- **Signal detection theory:** Multiple independent cues combine additively under default conditions unless there's explicit cue-interdependence. No cue-interdependence in reading Charm/Hope/Fear/Resonance — these are independent evidence channels. Additivity is the correct model.
- **Clinical attachment theory (TYPE analogy):** Secure vs anxious vs avoidant attachment are type distinctions, not magnitude distinctions. The clinical field demonstrates that TYPE has more predictive validity for behavioral outcomes than attachment intensity. Domain transfer validates P4-C.

### Extrapolation

- **Extrapolate specificity to extreme:** Maximally specific message = uniquely tailored to this person at this moment; impossible to have been written for anyone else. Signal cost at maximum. Predicts: maximum effective_magnitude. Real-world confirmation: highly researched personal outreach is famously effective.
- **Extrapolate TYPE over time:** Attachment TYPE is not static. A Hope-dominant (transactional) attachment can deepen to Resonance-dominant (bonded) if the interaction reveals shared understanding. TYPE evolution is a natural successor inquiry.
- **Extrapolate two-dimensionality:** If attachment has MAGNITUDE and TYPE, APT Profiling's natural destination is a 2D output space (not a scalar). PRAGMA and APT Inference should be moving toward this.

### P1 — Additive f Statement

**P1-A (Generic)**
> `f` is an additive function of its 4 variables:
>
> `f = a·charm + b·hope + c·fear + d·resonance`
>
> where coefficients a, b, c, d are context-dependent weighting factors (empirically underdetermined; professional contexts may weight Charm and Hope higher; friendship contexts may weight Resonance higher). Variables contribute independently; no interaction terms are claimed within `f`.
>
> Note on outer vs inner structure: the multiplicative gating `f × g₁ × g₂ × g₃` is a separate architectural claim about the relationship between f and the Modulator Suite. It does not imply any multiplicative structure within `f`. Inner additivity and outer multiplicativity are compatible; they operate at different levels.

*Test: Novelty — makes implicit explicit. Scrutiny survival — no test case contradicts additivity. Fertile — TYPE and specificity both build cleanly on this. Actionable — formula is usable. Mechanism independence — confirmed by multiple mechanisms.*

---

**P1-B (Focused)**
> The additive structure of `f` is the theory's explicitly held position, with evidence:
>
> `f = a·charm + b·hope + c·fear + d·resonance`
>
> Three alternative interaction structures were probed and eliminated:
>
> - **B1 (Resonance-as-credibility-gate):** Predicts a stranger offering $100K would fail to generate attachment without Resonance. Real-world: people accept $100K meetings from strangers. B1 killed.
> - **F1 (Combinatorial-bonus):** Predicts variable combinations produce MAGNITUDE above their additive sum. Reinterpreted: Resonance+Hope combinations produce richer attachment TYPE, not higher MAGNITUDE. F1 reinterpreted, not added to f.
> - **I1 (Negative-interaction-terms):** Predicts Fear+Hope without Resonance decreases attachment. Reinterpreted: this is a Coherence failure at g₂ (Model-Collapse modulator). No f-internal negative term needed.
>
> Parsimony principle applied: interaction terms must be evidence-justified; the burden of proof sits with future proposals. Three strong counter-examples were tested; additivity survived all.
>
> **Note on effective magnitude:** "additive in variables" means additive in their effective magnitudes. Each variable's effective magnitude includes a specificity factor (see §Signal Specificity). The additive claim is: `f = Σ(effective_magnitude_i × coefficient_i)`. This is still additive; specificity is a per-variable scalar, not an interaction term between variables.

*Test: More complete than P1-A; justification trail explicit. Preserves iteration-3.2 commitments. Most useful for a reader who might push back on additivity.*

---

**P1-C (Controversial)**
> Additive `f` is not a compromise with parsimony — it is the structurally correct description.
>
> Human attachment signal-reading operates via independent detection channels. Charm is read from status/competence signals. Hope is read from future-state offers. Fear is read from threat signals. Resonance is read from model-matching signals. These are distinct evidence classes processed through distinct cognitive subsystems. Independent channels integrate additively under default conditions (signal detection theory baseline). Cue-interdependence in the detection problem is required for multiplicative integration — there is no such interdependence here.
>
> `f = a·charm + b·hope + c·fear + d·resonance`
>
> The alternative — that Resonance gates the credibility of Hope — would require that the hope-detection subsystem is downstream of the resonance-detection subsystem. There is no evidence for this cognitive architecture. The person who offers $100K generates Hope signal through a separate channel from Resonance; the channels sum.
>
> Reformulation: **iteration-3.2's formula was already correct. Iteration-3.2.1 simply makes the inner structure explicit.**

*Controversial because: takes a stronger architectural claim (positive structural assertion, not just parsimony default). Useful if the theory needs to defend additive f against a theoretically sophisticated objector.*

### P2 — Signal Specificity

**P2-A (Generic)**
> **Signal specificity** is the degree to which a signal was tailored to this specific counterparty, as opposed to being sendable, with trivial modification, to any of N counterparties.
>
> Each variable's **effective magnitude** is:
>
> `effective_magnitude(variable_i) = nominal_content(variable_i) × specificity(signal)`
>
> A generic template has low specificity — its nominal content is discounted because it carries no evidence of genuine engagement with this person. A specific, tailored signal has high specificity — its nominal content is multiplied because it proves real investment.
>
> **Generic signal example:** "saw your product, let me know if you want to collaborate" — could be sent to any product-builder; specificity near-zero.
>
> **Specific signal example:** "saw your product post, fricking good idea" — names the product, demonstrates real engagement; specificity high.
>
> **Why specificity is not a 5th variable:** specificity fails the orthogonality test. It is not a distinct content-type — you cannot have "specificity" as a signal without specific CONTENT (Charm, Hope, Resonance, or Fear) to be specific about. Specificity is a quality of how those variables are expressed, not an independent attachment-generator.

---

**P2-B (Focused)**
> **Signal specificity as costly-signal.**
>
> Specific knowledge about a counterparty is expensive to acquire. Displaying it in a message proves investment — the message could not have been generated without genuine engagement. Generic templates are cheap signals; human cognitive systems discount cheap signals and amplify costly ones (evolutionary-psychology baseline).
>
> Operationalization for PRAGMA:
> - **Generic** = high template-match probability; content could apply to any counterparty in the same category
> - **Specific** = low template-match probability; content references the counterparty's particular attributes, work, stated concerns, or context
>
> Measurable indicators of specificity: (1) named-product/named-concept reference, (2) vocabulary match to their expressed domain, (3) reference to specific prior content (post, message, work product), (4) offer framed around their specific situation.
>
> `effective_magnitude(variable_i) = nominal_content(variable_i) × specificity(signal)`
>
> **Per-variable specificity:** specificity applies independently to each variable. A message can be high-specificity on Resonance (demonstrates shared domain) while low-specificity on Hope (generic collaboration offer). This allows fine-grained analysis. Example: "I love the space you're building in [specific insight about domain] — let me know if you want to connect" has high Resonance-specificity, low Hope-specificity.
>
> **Why not a 5th variable:** specificity has no independent attachment contribution at zero content. Pure specificity without content — a message that is "tailored" but says nothing — generates no attachment. A 5th variable must generate attachment independently; specificity doesn't.

---

**P2-C (Controversial)**
> **Specificity may be a credibility threshold, not a linear multiplier.**
>
> Standard formulation: `effective_magnitude = nominal × specificity` — where specificity scales smoothly from 0 to 1.
>
> Counter-hypothesis: below a minimum specificity threshold θ, a signal doesn't register as a weaker version of itself — it registers as noise and is discarded.
>
> `effective_magnitude = nominal × specificity    if specificity ≥ θ`
> `effective_magnitude = 0                        if specificity < θ`
>
> Evidence: Person B generates *zero* registered attachment, not *weak* attachment. If specificity were a linear multiplier, Person B's template (with some nominal Hope content) would still produce a small, non-zero Hope signal. The actual outcome — zero interest, zero response — suggests the template crossed below a threshold and was discarded, not just discounted.
>
> Real-world parallel: in spam filtering, a message below a credibility threshold is not "a weak email" — it is no email. The threshold behavior is not a gradation of the linear model; it's a categorical classification.
>
> **Practical implication:** there is no "improve your template by 20%" path. The threshold model says you must get above θ or you're invisible. This makes the theory prescriptively sharper — and more honest about the real phenomenology of generic outreach.
>
> **Status for spec:** iteration-3.2.1 uses the linear model as the default (simpler, defensible, explains all test cases at sufficient precision). The threshold model is flagged as a frontier question for iteration-3.3 empirical testing.

### P3 — Sender-Side SP from Message Style

**P3-A (Generic)**
> Iteration-3.2 established that the Modulator Suite (g₁ × g₂ × g₃) applies to the sender as perceived by the receiver. Self-Positioning (g₁) is the sender's displayed state — High-Self-Focus or Supplication. In **single-message contexts**, where no longitudinal track record is available, the **message style itself is the primary carrier of the sender's modulator signal.**
>
> - A **template/generic message** displays Supplication: the sender is fishing, approval-seeking, structuring the message as a low-investment request. Receiver reads this as low sender-SP. Sender-g₁ → low.
>
> - A **specific/engaged message** displays Self-Focus: the sender is expressing their own evaluation, making a concrete offer with clear agenda, demonstrating genuine engagement. Receiver reads this as high sender-SP. Sender-g₁ → high.
>
> This is not a new mechanism. It is the existing iteration-3.2 modulator framework applied explicitly to the case where only one message is available for reading. In multi-interaction contexts, Coherence (g₂) and longitudinal behavior build the modulator picture over time. In single-message contexts, style is the only available signal.

---

**P3-B (Focused)**
> **The receiver applies their g-function to the sender** — this is the architectural center of iteration-3.2's modulator system. When a receiver reads a message, they simultaneously parse content (contributing to f) and evaluate the sender as a person (contributing to g₁ × g₂ × g₃, applied to sender). The sender's total attachment-generation potential:
>
> `Attachment_generated ≈ f(C,H,F,R) × g₁(sender-SP) × g₂(sender-Coherence) × g₃(sender-EC)`
>
> In single-message text contexts (Reddit DM, LinkedIn, email):
> - `g₁` (SP) is directly readable from message tone and structure
> - `g₂` (Coherence) is partially readable: internal contradictions within the message are available; longitudinal track record is not
> - `g₃` (EC) is minimally readable from text alone
>
> **The double-collapse mechanism:** A generic template depresses attachment through TWO independent failure paths simultaneously:
>
> 1. **f-layer failure:** low specificity → low effective_magnitude across all variables → low f-sum
> 2. **g-layer failure:** Supplication-displaying style → low sender-g₁ → low g-product
>
> Person B's near-zero attachment results from both paths firing at once. This is not "Hope without Resonance fails" — it is f collapsed AND g collapsed, both caused by the same single choice to send a generic template.
>
> **Practical annotation:** in single-message contexts, specificity and Self-Focus-display are mechanically linked. A genuinely specific message is almost always written from a Self-Focus state (the sender cared enough to engage). A generic template almost always displays Supplication (the sender is fishing, not expressing conviction). The two mechanisms are correlated in practice but architecturally independent.

---

**P3-C (Controversial)**
> **The message style IS the Self-Positioning act — not evidence of it.**
>
> Standard framing: message style allows the receiver to *infer* the sender's Self-Positioning state. The receiver reads the signal and reconstructs the underlying state.
>
> Alternative framing: in interpersonal contexts, how you write IS your Self-Focus. The display is not downstream of the state — it is the state. Self-Focus does not exist as a hidden internal entity that gets expressed through behavior; it is constituted by behavior.
>
> Evidence for the alternative: the sender who writes "fricking good idea" is not performing enthusiasm while secretly indifferent. Their attention is genuinely on their own evaluation of the product. The sender who writes "let me know if you want to collaborate" is not performing Supplication while secretly confident. Their attention is genuinely on whether they'll get a response.
>
> **Practical implication:** there is no cognitive shortcut. Writing a "Self-Focused-seeming" template without genuine engagement produces incongruent signals — vocabulary and structure from a SP-display template but no underlying product knowledge to fill in specific content. Receivers detect this incongruence (g₂ Coherence failure — internal contradiction within the message). The only path to high sender-g₁ is actual Self-Focus, which generates natural specific content.
>
> **Relationship to P5-C:** this mechanism is what makes the prescriptive conclusion in P5-C work. The theory doesn't say "appear specific" — it says genuine engagement is structurally required.

### P4 — Attachment MAGNITUDE vs TYPE

**P4-A (Generic)**
> Attachment has two output dimensions: **MAGNITUDE** and **TYPE**.
>
> **MAGNITUDE** is the scalar quantity — how strongly the observer is drawn to engage. Produced by `f(C,H,F,R) × g₁ × g₂ × g₃`. Higher magnitude = stronger behavioral engagement drive.
>
> **TYPE** is the qualitative character — what kind of engagement it generates. TYPE emerges directly from the variable mix inside `f` — which variable dominated the weighted sum.
>
> **Illustrative TYPE taxonomy (provisional — not exhaustive):**
>
> | Dominant variable(s) | TYPE | Behavioral signature | Persistence |
> |---|---|---|---|
> | Charm-dominant | Status/admiration | Seeks proximity to signal association | Weakens at distance or when access ends |
> | Hope-dominant | Transactional | Conditional engagement; outcome-focused | Dissolves when exchange ends or offer lapses |
> | Fear-dominant | Coerced | Compliance-driven; resentment latent | Reverses (relief, not attachment) when threat removed |
> | Resonance-dominant | Bonded/deep | Persistent; survives cost and distance | Maintained through shared model in memory |
> | Charm + Resonance | Respected leader | Follows and admires; driven by both elevation and alignment | Moderate-high |
> | Hope + Resonance | Collaborative partner | Transacts AND connects; sees long-term | High |
>
> *Taxonomy is illustrative. Empirical derivation and cross-cultural validation pending (iteration-3.3 scope).*
>
> **MAGNITUDE and TYPE are independent:** same MAGNITUDE can have radically different TYPE. TYPE determines behavioral trajectory; MAGNITUDE determines current behavioral intensity.

---

**P4-B (Focused)**
> **Why MAGNITUDE and TYPE are not reducible to each other:**
>
> Consider two people generating equal attachment scores (`f × g = 0.6`) but different variable mixes:
>
> - **Person α:** `f(charm=0.5, hope=0.1, fear=0, resonance=0)` — Charm-dominant
> - **Person β:** `f(charm=0, hope=0.1, fear=0, resonance=0.5)` — Resonance-dominant
>
> Same MAGNITUDE (0.6). Completely different TYPE. Person α generates admiration attachment that weakens when Charm access is removed (celebrity retires, audience shrinks). Person β generates bonded attachment that persists through absence and cost because the shared model lives in the observer's memory.
>
> MAGNITUDE predicts: how much behavioral engagement *now*. TYPE predicts: how behavioral engagement evolves *over time* and under *changed conditions*.
>
> **Persistence dynamics are TYPE-dependent:**
> - **Resonance-dominant:** survives loss of access. Shared model persists in memory; observer doesn't require continued interaction to maintain attachment.
> - **Hope-dominant:** survives only if hope is continually renewed. Exchange-conditioned. Ends when exchange ends.
> - **Charm-dominant:** access-dependent. Weakens as the charm source recedes.
> - **Fear-dominant:** reverses on removal. Relief replaces compliance. Often followed by aversion.
>
> **Architectural elegance:** Because `f` is additive, TYPE reads directly from the variable-weight distribution — no separate TYPE-extraction mechanism is needed. The additive structure makes TYPE a first-order output.

---

**P4-C (Controversial)**
> **TYPE is the more diagnostically important dimension. MAGNITUDE should be reported as context, not the primary output.**
>
> Standard reporting framing: "How much attachment does this person generate?" (MAGNITUDE first). TYPE is the label added afterward.
>
> Inversion: the primary diagnostic question should be "What kind of attachment does this person generate?" (TYPE first). MAGNITUDE provides context for how intensely that TYPE is currently expressed.
>
> **Evidence from adjacent domains:**
> - In clinical attachment theory, secure/anxious/avoidant TYPE has more predictive validity for behavioral outcomes than attachment intensity
> - In sales: Hope-dominant client retention is significantly lower than Resonance-dominant retention regardless of engagement intensity at point of close
> - In leadership: Fear-dominant followership produces compliance but collapses on threat removal; Resonance-dominant followership produces voluntary advocacy
>
> In every domain where tracking the relationship over time matters, TYPE outperforms MAGNITUDE as a predictor.
>
> **Implication for APT Profiling output:** primary field → `attachment_type_tendency`; secondary field → `attachment_magnitude`. "This person tends to generate Resonance+Hope mixed attachment [TYPE] at moderate intensity [MAGNITUDE]" is more actionable than a scalar.
>
> **Status for spec:** iteration-3.2.1 introduces both dimensions as co-equal. The TYPE-primary claim is held here as a frontier position for operator/practitioner guidance. The formal spec doesn't rank dimensions — reporting systems can prioritize per use-case.

**P5-C (Controversial)**
> **What would Person B need to write to generate what Person A generated?**
>
> Inverse analysis — prescriptive direction:
>
> Person B's failure has two components. Fixing both:
>
> **F-layer fix (P2):** Replace generic content with specific content.
> - Requirement: know one specific thing about the product that genuinely struck you

> - Requirement: have a genuine reason to want the meeting
> - "Let me know if you want to" → "I want to explore [specific overlap]. Meeting [specific day]?"
> - Cost: actual conviction that this conversation is worth your time
>

>
> Analysis: Specificity high → effective magnitudes high. Self-Focus structure → sender-g₁ high. MAGNITUDE: substantial. TYPE: collaborative/bonded.
>
> **The key structural insight this reveals:** there is no optimization path for the template. The F-layer fix requires genuine product engagement (you must actually know something specific). The G-layer fix requires genuine conviction (you must actually want the meeting). Templates can't acquire either property through wordsmithing — P3-C's mechanism says the display IS the state; incongruent templates are readable as incongruent.
>
> **Theory prescribes authenticity not as a virtue but as a structural requirement.**
