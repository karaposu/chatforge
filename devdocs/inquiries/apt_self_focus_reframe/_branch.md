# Branch: apt_self_focus_reframe

## Question

The existing finding (`devdocs/inquiries/apt_missing_dimension/finding.md`) names the new APT dimension **Self-Positioning** with **Self-Elevation** as its internal axis and five-signal external correlates. The user proposes a reframe: the root concept is actually **Self-Focus (*kendine odaklanmak*)** — where your attention genuinely lives — with **Displayed Self-Focus (*kendine odaklanmanın gözükmesi*)** as the visibility requirement. Under this reframe, Self-Elevation is a *state*, while Self-Focus is a *causal mechanism* — attention genuinely elsewhere *produces* scarce investment, unmanaged reactions, no over-giving, and amplified charm/hope/fear, without being strategic or performed. Is the Self-Focus reframe materially better than the Self-Positioning framing for the APT integration (name + mechanism + architectural placement + PRAGMA inference + diagnostic), equal with different surface vocabulary, or worse in a way that should be rejected — and if better, what specifically changes in the finding and in the surgical update to `apt_layer.md`?

## Goal

A clear verdict on whether to:

- **(A) Replace** the Self-Positioning framing with Self-Focus at the umbrella level — and specifically update `finding.md`'s name choice, mechanism description, trait structure, signal catalog interpretation, and 2×2 typology naming.
- **(B) Adopt Self-Focus as the primary mechanism explanation** while keeping Self-Positioning as the umbrella label (or swap: make Self-Focus the umbrella, Self-Elevation a sub-axis or downstream consequence).
- **(C) Reject** the reframe — keep the existing finding intact — with structural reasoning for why Self-Focus fails where Self-Positioning holds.

The verdict should be precise enough to either (i) edit the specific lines in `finding.md` that change, or (ii) write a short "considered but rejected" note in the existing finding's Reasoning section.

## Scope Check

Question covers goal: YES.

The question explicitly asks for a three-way verdict (Replace / Adopt-as-mechanism / Reject) and the downstream edits that follow. The goal asks for precisely that decision with enough specificity to edit `finding.md`. Answering the question fully answers the goal.

One nuance that sharpens rather than widens the scope: the prior inquiry found that "Visible-Need Concealment" (Innovation candidate 3.B, Inversion Focused) was a *deeper mechanism* than Self-Elevation — and Critique accepted it as a MEDIUM-confidence supplementary mechanism. Self-Focus is closely related to but distinct from Visible-Need Concealment:
- Self-Focus says: attention is *on your own priorities* (positive-directional — where focus is)
- Visible-Need Concealment says: *visible need* is minimized (negative-directional — what's not visible)

Self-Focus might be the *upstream cause* that produces low visible-need as a consequence. This means the Self-Focus proposal partly aligns with an earlier finding that was already accepted as supplementary. The question is whether that earlier finding should be *promoted* from supplementary to primary, displacing Self-Elevation.

**The tension to resolve:**

The user's proposal has real structural appeal:
- **Causal clarity:** "attention is elsewhere" explains *why* investment is calibrated low, why displeasure shows freely, why charm isn't needy. Self-Elevation describes the posture without naming the mechanism.
- **Non-prescriptive framing:** "Self-focus" sounds less like "be arrogant / be high-status" than "Self-Elevation," which the finding already flagged as a risk (R1 in sensemaking's risk perspective: "reads as prescriptive arrogance").
- **Alignment with earlier findings:** Innovation's 3.B already identified visible-need-level as possibly deeper than Self-Elevation; Critique accepted as supplementary. The user is now asking whether that supplementary theory should be primary.

But it also has concerns:
- **Does Self-Focus explain the devaluation mechanism?** The finding's core claim is that without the modulator, high charm/hope/fear gets *devalued post-hoc* ("this guy doesn't deserve this"). Does "attention is elsewhere" explain why charm gets discounted? Possibly: scarce-attention charm feels background-processed, not desperate; desperate charm (from self-focused-on-them) signals low status. But this chain needs tightening.
- **Does Self-Focus need Self-Elevation underneath?** Someone could be self-focused because *anxious* and distracted — not self-elevated. If anxious-distracted reads the same as self-elevated in behavior, but only the latter produces the desired effect, then Self-Focus without Self-Elevation underneath fails. The reframe might be pointing at a downstream proxy.
- **Does "Displayed Self-Focus" collapse back toward costly signaling?** The visibility requirement (gözükmesi) makes Self-Focus observable. But *visible* self-focus = a cost-signal = the costly-signaling theory already captured. So the reframe might not be adding; it might be renaming.
- **Architectural placement:** if Self-Focus replaces Self-Elevation as the axis, the Path α architecture holds unchanged (umbrella concept + internal axis + external correlates). Only the vocabulary changes. If Self-Focus replaces Self-Positioning as the umbrella too, trait structure and 2×2 diagnostic also shift.

Candidate outcomes for critique to adjudicate:

- **(A) Full replacement:** Self-Focus is the umbrella; "Displayed Self-Focus" (what the other sees) is the external-correlate layer; Self-Elevation is demoted to "one posture that produces visible self-focus, among others" — possibly dropped entirely.
- **(B) Mechanism promotion:** Keep Self-Positioning as the umbrella (it signals the *structural position* in the theory — a modulator on attachment). Inside it, replace Self-Elevation with Self-Focus as the internal axis — naming the mechanism instead of the state. External correlates stay the same.
- **(C) Surface-only swap:** Self-Focus and Self-Elevation describe the same phenomenon from different angles (cause vs state). Use Self-Focus as the intuitive description; keep Self-Elevation as the formal axis label. No structural change.
- **(D) Reject:** Self-Focus is a downstream proxy (visible-need-concealment already captured it as supplementary). Promoting it to primary loses the Self-Elevation insight that internal hierarchy-posture matters, not just attention direction.
