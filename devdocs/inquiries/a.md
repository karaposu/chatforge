How to read them together

  - Self-Positioning is a MODULATOR in APT's architecture. It sits between Attachment and Presentation. This is its structural-role name.
  - Non-Extractive Attention is the AXIS inside Self-Positioning. It's the genus that contains multiple species.
  - Self-Focus is one SPECIES of Non-Extractive Attention — specifically, the one most relevant for value-establishing contexts (LinkedIn,
   dating, professional outreach). Other species: Caregiver-Focus, Mission-Focus, Task-Focus, Dialogue-Focus, Transcendent-Focus.
