## Phase 3 — Ambiguity Collapse

### Ambiguity 1 — Resonance: modulator or 4th attachment variable?

**The defining test:** Can Resonance generate attachment WITHOUT any Charm/Hope/Fear present?

**Scenario:** Two strangers meet and discover a deeply unusual shared interest (say, medieval Ottoman financial instruments). Instant connection. No status games (both non-experts). No benefit-seeking (non-commercial). No fear-based hold (strangers). Just mutual recognition of the uncommon interest.

**Does attachment form?** YES — attested by friendship literature, "soul-connection" reports, intellectual partnerships, shared-niche communities.

**Does Charm/Hope/Fear mediate it?** NO — the connection forms via recognition itself, bypassing the three.

**Resolution:** **Resonance is a 4th attachment variable, NOT a modulator.**

**Structural consequence:** Domain 1 expands from 3 to 4 variables. Updated formula:

```
Attachment ≈ f(charm, hope, fear, resonance) × g₁(SP) × g₂(Coherence) × g₃(Emotional Composure)
```

Resonance operates additively within f (can generate attachment alone); modulators operate multiplicatively on f (can zero out attachment regardless of f).

**Counter-interpretation test:** Could Resonance be a modulator that amplifies f only when f is non-zero (multiplies by >1)? If so, at f=0, Resonance's multiplier × 0 = 0; attachment should be zero. But attested phenomena say attachment forms at f=0 via Resonance alone. Counter-interpretation fails.

**What's fixed:** Domain 1 has 4 variables. Resonance operates additively in f.
**What's no longer allowed:** Classifying Resonance as a modulator; treating Domain 1 as 3-variable.
**Depends on:** PRAGMA → Domain 1 mapping updates; APT Inference Resonance inference; APT Profiling Resonance bearing.

### Ambiguity 2 — Vitality: threshold-modulator or additive factor?

**The defining test:** Does Vitality behave multiplicatively across its full range, or only at thresholds?

**Analysis:**
- Normal range (slight fatigue to moderate depletion): signals are slightly discounted. 10% tired → ~10% signal reduction. ADDITIVE behavior.
- Extreme range (dissociation, severe clinical depletion): signals collapse entirely regardless of other factors. Multiplicative zero.

**Verdict:** Vitality is a SWITCHING mechanism, not a smooth modulator. Modulators in APT operate multiplicatively across their range (Self-Positioning's gating works at any Self-Focus level, not just at collapse threshold).

**Analog:** electrical fuse — below threshold, current passes (additive); above threshold, circuit opens (switching). Fuses aren't modulators.

**Resolution:** Vitality is NOT a Suite member. Documented as "degradation factor with modulator-like behavior at clinical extremes (dissociation, severe depletion); additive in normal ranges."

**Counter-interpretation test:** Could we loosen modulator criteria to include threshold behavior? This would admit Vitality but would also open the door for other threshold-dependent factors (clinical depression? anxiety disorders? substance intoxication?). Unprincipled criterion loosening.

**What's fixed:** Vitality is not a modulator. The Modulator Suite has 3 members, not 4.
**What's no longer allowed:** Threshold-only multiplicativity as sufficient for modulator status.
**Depends on:** Vitality documented as degradation factor in a brief note, not a Suite section.

### Ambiguity 3 — Cluster 4 trigger: are the attention-aspects of new modulators load-bearing?

**Coherence — what's load-bearing?**

Coherence has three aspects: attention-consistency (does your attention stay on same priorities over time?) + identity/values-coherence (who you actually are consistently) + alignment-coherence (surface matches depth).

**Dissociation test:** Can you be attention-consistent but identity-incoherent?

Yes — a consistent manipulator has attention consistently on their scheme but identity is incoherent (they say one thing, believe another; values shift opportunistically). Observer reads the identity-incoherence when they catch contradictions; attachment collapses via Model-Collapse.

Can you be identity-coherent but attention-inconsistent? Yes — a principled person whose attention drifts but values are stable. Attachment can hold because identity is stable despite drift.

**Load-bearing aspect: IDENTITY/INTEGRITY, not attention.**

**Emotional Composure — what's load-bearing?**

Aspects: attention-under-stress + physiological regulation + affective regulation + recovery speed.

**Dissociation test:** Can you be attentionally composed but physiologically dysregulated?

Yes — mental composure with shaking voice / erratic breathing / visible body tension. Observer reads the physiology via 100+ milliseconds of perception; attachment discounts or collapses via Contagion-Drain.

Can you be physiologically composed but attentionally panicking internally? Hypothetically yes, but physiology leaks — hard to hold physiology while attention is scrambled. And if somehow you could, observer attaches to the composed physiology.

**Load-bearing aspect: PHYSIOLOGICAL REGULATION, not attention.**

**Cluster 4 verdict:** Both surviving modulators have load-bearing aspects that are NON-attention. Cluster 4 reopening condition ("multiple modulators all attention-based") is NOT triggered.

**Counter-interpretation test:** Could we read these modulators as attention-based despite the dissociation tests? Only by redefining "attention" to include identity and physiology, which is overreach. The word "attention" has specific meaning in cognitive science; overloading it destroys the concept.

**What's fixed:** Cluster 4 NOT triggered by this inquiry. iteration-4 substrate reframe remains planned-for-future, unchanged in status.
**What's no longer allowed:** Claiming this inquiry's findings force substrate reframe.
**Depends on:** iteration-4 substrate reframe inquiry timing (unchanged); Cluster 4 reopening conditions (unchanged).

### Ambiguity 4 — Architectural integration: separate modulators or Modulator Suite umbrella?

**Options:**
- (a) Three separate named modulators, each with own top-level section
- (b) "Modulator Suite" umbrella concept containing three members
- (c) Hierarchical — Self-Positioning primary, others secondary

All three:

- Have dual character (same architectural property)

- Are orthogonal to each other (same structural relationship)

Structurally equal. Self-Positioning was discovered first; it's not architecturally privileged.

**Resolution:** **Option (b) — "Modulator Suite" umbrella with 3 structurally-equal members.**

**Counter-interpretation:** Could Self-Positioning be architecturally primary because it was discovered first or because it covers the most ground (6 species)? Discovery-order isn't a structural argument. Ground-coverage differs between modulators but doesn't privilege any.

**What's fixed:** Modulator Suite as umbrella architectural concept. 3 members currently, structurally equal.
**What's no longer allowed:** Treating Self-Positioning as architecturally privileged.
**Depends on:** new_apt_layer.md structure; APT Inference output structure.

### Ambiguity 5 — Coherence sub-structure: aspects or species?

**Question:** Does Coherence have a genus/species taxonomy like Self-Positioning?

**Self-Positioning species test:** Self-Focus, Caregiver-Focus, Mission-Focus etc. are distinct MODES — the attention direction is categorically different between them. Species have different mechanisms of operation.

**Coherence test:** Stability Coherence and Alignment Coherence are different ASPECTS that can co-occur in various combinations, but they don't operate by categorically different mechanisms. Both contribute to the same observer-inference ("is this person consistent and authentic?"). They're facets of one property, not distinct modes.

**Verdict:** Coherence has ASPECTS (Stability + Alignment), not species. No genus/species taxonomy.

**What's fixed:** Coherence is structurally simpler than Self-Positioning. Aspects, not species.
**Depends on:** Coherence section structure in new_apt_layer.md.

### Ambiguity 6 — Emotional Composure sub-structure: aspects or species?

Same analysis as Ambiguity 5. Sub-aspects (Physiological regulation, Affective regulation, Recovery speed) are facets that co-occur, not distinct modes with different mechanisms.

**Verdict:** Emotional Composure has ASPECTS (Physiological + Affective + Recovery), not species.

**Only Self-Positioning has genus/species taxonomy** — because attention has genuine directional types (self, other, mission, task, dialogue, transcendent) that operate through different mechanisms. Coherence and Emotional Composure are simpler: one property with multiple observable facets.
