# Finding: apt_modulator_landscape

**This finding is iteration-3.2 of the APT theory — an architectural expansion of iteration-3.1.** It supersedes `self_focus_dynamics/finding.md` at the architectural level (Modulator Suite umbrella + Domain 1 expansion to 4 variables). All iteration-3.1 operational-dynamics content (outcome-independence central concept, tiered calibration audit, multimodal metrics for Self-Positioning, species-context-fit framework, practice protocol references, oscillation dynamics, character-perception scoping) is preserved within the updated architecture.

iteration-3.2's central architectural move: APT moves from a **one-modulator theory** (Self-Positioning alone, iterations 1 through 3.1) to a **multi-modulator theory** via the **Modulator Suite** — a named architectural slot currently containing 3 structurally-equal members (at the role level). Alongside, Domain 1 expands from 3 attachment variables (Charm/Hope/Fear) to 4 (adding Resonance as a dyadic-emergent attachment generator).

This is the biggest iteration jump since iteration-3.0. All iteration-1 through iteration-3.1 commitments are preserved unchanged.

## Finding

### The Modulator Suite — a new architectural concept

APT's modulator layer is renamed and expanded. What iteration-1 called "Self-Positioning" as a single modulator is now understood as **one member of the Modulator Suite** — a named architectural slot containing multiple modulators, all operating at the same structural position (between Domain 1 and Domain 2) with the same architectural properties (multiplicative gating, dual character, distinct failure signatures, orthogonality to each other).

The Suite currently has **3 structurally-equal members:**

1. **Self-Positioning** (from iteration-1 through iteration-3.1, preserved in full) — genus: Non-Extractive Attention; primary species: Self-Focus + Caregiver-Focus; secondary species: Mission-Focus, Task-Focus, Dialogue-Focus, Transcendent-Focus; negative pole: Supplication; failure signature: **devaluation**; central property: outcome-independence
2. **Coherence** (new in iteration-3.2) — aspects: Stability + Alignment; failure signature: **Model-Collapse**
3. **Emotional Composure** (new in iteration-3.2) — aspects: Physiological + Affective + Recovery; failure signature: **Contagion-Drain**

"Structurally equal at role level" — all three are modulators with the same architectural properties. "Temporal-detection differences" — Self-Positioning and Emotional Composure have INSTANTANEOUS signal catalogs (readable in seconds); Coherence requires LONGITUDINAL signal aggregation (cross-segment observation over time). This is an operational-detection difference, not a role-difference.

The Modulator Suite has a unifying theme — **reliability** — that all three members track (predictable non-extractive attention; consistent person; regulated emotional state). This is conceptual unity, not mechanism-unity. The 3 modulators have DISTINCT FAILURE SIGNATURES; collapsing them into "Reliability" would lose diagnostic differentiation that observers experience naturally.

### Domain 1 expansion — 4 attachment variables

Domain 1 expands from 3 attachment variables (Charm, Hope, Fear) to 4 with the addition of **Resonance** — genuine mutual recognition / shared world-model / "you get me" — emerging dyadically when two parties' internal maps align enough to register recognition.

Resonance is **not a modulator.** It's an attachment variable operating additively within the f-function. The decisive test: Resonance can generate attachment WITHOUT any Charm/Hope/Fear present (the shared-niche-interest case — two strangers discover an unusual shared interest and attach via recognition alone, no status games, no benefit-seeking, no fear). Multiplicative modulators cannot do this (multiplicative × 0 = 0). Therefore Resonance operates additively in f.

Resonance is distinct from Dialogue-Focus species (of Self-Positioning). Dialogue-Focus is an **individual species** — one party's attention is on shared inquiry. Resonance is a **dyadic emergent** — emerges when both parties' values/worldviews/attention-objects align. When both parties are in Dialogue-Focus on the same object, Resonance emerges; but Dialogue-Focus can operate solo (without Resonance), and Resonance can emerge in other species combinations if values align.

### Updated multiplicative gating formula

```
Attachment ≈ f(charm, hope, fear, resonance) × g₁(SP) × g₂(Coherence) × g₃(Emotional Composure)
```

Each `g` is a modulator's gating function. Any modulator can independently collapse attachment — if any g → 0, the whole product collapses. Each failure has a distinct signature (devaluation / Model-Collapse / Contagion-Drain), which is diagnostic — observers perceive which modulator has failed.

### Coherence — complete specification

**Definition:** Coherence is the consistency of a person's non-extractive-attention expression across time and contexts (Stability aspect) combined with the alignment between surface expression and internal state (Alignment aspect). Observers infer Coherence from longitudinal observation; when it fails, observers cannot form a stable mental model of the person → attachment cannot consolidate regardless of other signals.

**Aspects (not species — shared mechanism, different content):**
- **Stability Coherence** — same person across time and across different contexts (work, home, social, crisis)
- **Alignment Coherence** — surface expression matches internal state (what you say/do matches what you believe/feel)

**Failure signature — Model-Collapse:** Observer's attempt to form a stable predictive model of the person fails due to inconsistencies or misalignments. Attachment discounts as a result. Observer reports: "I can't figure out who they really are" / "They're different every time I see them" / "Something doesn't add up."

**Orthogonality (dissociation tests — theoretical orthogonality, not empirical independence):**
- **vs Self-Positioning:** Narcissists are often Self-Focused at moment-to-moment but incoherent across contexts (this is the Coherence-based refinement of iteration-3's narcissism reconciliation — the narcissist displays Self-Focus but is INCOHERENT, which is what gets detected over time as the mimicry framework also described); a consistent, caring mother is not-Self-Focused but highly Coherent
- **vs Emotional Composure:** A consistent hothead is Coherent but dysregulated; a calm liar is Composed but incoherent

**Signal Catalog (3-tier longitudinal structure):**

*Inference signals (longitudinal — require cross-segment observation):*
- **Cross-Context Stability** — same core behavior and priorities across work/home/social
- **Value-Action Alignment** — stated values match observed actions
- **Temporal Self-Consistency** — same person over weeks/months (not wild fluctuations)
- **Role-Boundary Consistency** — roles taken on and dropped consistently
- **Commitment-Follow-Through** — stated intentions followed through

*Text-feature metrics (cross-message longitudinal aggregation):*
- Value-vocabulary consistency across messages
- Topic-continuity across conversations
- Tone consistency (not shifting dramatically per audience)

*Multimodal signatures (longitudinal):*
- Posture/voice consistency across video calls over time
- Dress/grooming consistency (subject to context-appropriateness)

**PRAGMA → Coherence mapping:** Requires cross-segment aggregation rather than per-message inference. Dialogic Function patterns aggregated over time; Information Density consistency; cross-context comparison needed.

**Cultivation references** (one-line pointers, not framework imports):

### Emotional Composure — complete specification

**Definition:** Emotional Composure is the capacity to regulate emotional and physiological state under pressure — staying present and functional when stressed, challenged, or disrupted. Includes physiological regulation (body, voice, breath), affective regulation (emotional valence), and recovery speed (bouncing back from disruption).

**Aspects (not species — shared mechanism, different content):**
- **Physiological Regulation** — body, voice, breath under stress
- **Affective Regulation** — emotional valence stability
- **Recovery Speed** — time to return to baseline after disruption

**Failure signature — Contagion-Drain:** Dysregulation transfers emotional load to observer. Observer feels drained, burdened, or uncomfortable — as if their own emotional state is being affected by proximity to the dysregulated person. Attachment signals discount or collapse. Observer reports: "They're exhausting to be around" / "I feel worse after talking with them" / "Too much drama."

**Orthogonality (dissociation tests):**
- **vs Self-Positioning:** Anxious-Self-Focused person (inward-directed but dysregulated); calm-Caregiver-Focused person (composed while attending to other)
- **vs Coherence:** Consistent hothead (Coherent + dysregulated); calm liar (Composed + incoherent)

**Signal Catalog (3-tier instantaneous structure — parallel to Self-Positioning):**

*Inference signals (instantaneous, 5):*
- **Voice Stability Under Challenge** — pitch and pace stability when questioned or disagreed with
- **Recovery Latency** — time from disruption to baseline
- **Affect-Content Congruence** — emotional tone matches content (not forced or performed)
- **Physiological Leakage** — absence of shaking/tension/rushed breath under stress
- **De-escalation Capacity** — ability to stay composed when the other party escalates

*Text-feature metrics (stress-responsive):*
- Word choice under pressure (calm vs reactive)
- Response-latency patterns when challenged
- Punctuation/capitalization under stress
- Escalation vs de-escalation language in message threads

*Multimodal metrics (instantaneous):*
- Breath pattern under stress
- Body tension patterns
- Eye contact regulation (not freezing or darting)
- Voice pitch regulation
- Gesture regulation (not manic or frozen)

**PRAGMA → Emotional Composure mapping:** Temporal Structure signals (response-latency variance under stress); affect-laden Dialogic Function patterns; voice/video analysis for multimodal.

**Cultivation references:**
- **James Gross's process model of emotion regulation** — cognitive reappraisal, expressive suppression, situation selection/modification
- **Stephen Porges's polyvagal theory** — ventral vagal (social engagement) vs sympathetic vs dorsal vagal states
- **Peter Levine's Somatic Experiencing** — completion of trauma/stress cycles via body-based practices

### Resonance — 4th attachment variable specification

**Definition:** Resonance is genuine mutual recognition between two parties — sharing world-models, values, frameworks, or ways of seeing. It emerges **dyadically** when both parties' internal maps align enough for "you get me" to register. Resonance is NOT a property of either individual alone; it's a property of the pair.

**Role:** 4th attachment variable in Domain 1, alongside Charm, Hope, Fear. Generates attachment additively within the f-function (not multiplicatively as a modulator). Can generate attachment even when Charm/Hope/Fear = 0 (the decisive classification test).

**Operational signals (for PRAGMA detection):**
- Agreement rate (spontaneous, not social-obligation)
- Language convergence (both parties start using each other's terms)
- Shared-reference comfort (both can allude to the same things without explanation)
- Non-explicit mutual understanding ("you know what I mean" landing correctly)
- Co-construction patterns in Dialogic Function

**PRAGMA → Resonance mapping:** Convergent cross-dyad signals. Dialogic Function patterns showing co-construction; Information Density match; language-model convergence over conversation.

**Structural distinction from Dialogue-Focus species:**
- Dialogue-Focus = one party's individual species (attention on shared inquiry) — individual property
- Resonance = dyadic emergent attachment variable — relational property

When both parties are in Dialogue-Focus on the same object → Resonance emerges. But Dialogue-Focus can operate solo (one party), and Resonance can emerge in other species combinations if values align. These interact but aren't reducible to each other.

### Vitality — documented degradation factor (NOT Suite member)

Vitality (physical presence / aliveness / energy level) is **not a Modulator Suite member**. It behaves as a **threshold/switching factor**:

- **Normal range** (slight fatigue, everyday depletion): additive degradation — signals are slightly discounted (~10% tired → ~10% signal reduction)
- **Clinical extremes** (dissociation, severe depletion): multiplicative collapse — signals are zeroed regardless of other factors

Modulators in APT's architecture operate **smoothly multiplicatively across their range**. Threshold-switching behavior doesn't qualify. Admitting it would open criterion-loosening paths (clinical depression? substance intoxication? severe sleep deprivation?) without principled gating.

**Implication:** Vitality is documented in a brief "Degradation Factors" subsection — acknowledged as real and impactful at clinical extremes, but not promoted to Suite membership. Different architectural category.

## Reasoning

### Why the Modulator Suite architectural concept

iteration-1 introduced "Self-Positioning" as "the" named modulator. The slot-pattern was clear — modulators sit between Domain 1 and Domain 2 — but with only one occupant, the architectural category was unnamed. When the user asked whether other modulators exist, exploration canvased 27 candidates and qualified 2 more. With multiple modulators confirmed, the architectural slot needs a name.

**Modulator Suite** captures three things:
1. The architectural slot (modulators between Domain 1 and Domain 2)
2. The category of architectural elements (modulators are a type of thing; specific modulators are members)
3. Extensibility (future candidates go here if they pass gatekeeping criteria)

Without the Suite umbrella, with 3 modulators, there'd be architectural ambiguity — is Self-Positioning privileged because it was first? Is there a hierarchy? Suite clarifies: all members are structurally equal at role level; no privilege.

### Why 3 structurally-equal members (not hierarchical)

A hierarchical reading — Self-Positioning as primary, Coherence and EC as secondary — was considered and rejected. All three:
- Have multiplicative gating (same mechanism type)
- Have dual character (same architectural property)
- Have distinct failure signatures (different content, same structural role)
- Are orthogonal to each other (same structural relationship)
- Operate at the same architectural level

Structurally equal. Discovery-order is historical, not architectural. Self-Positioning appeared first because it was the most immediately visible modulator in the user's LinkedIn case; that doesn't make it architecturally primary.

### Why Coherence qualifies as a modulator

Coherence passes the 8 qualification criteria rigorously:

1. **Multiplicative on attachment:** YES — erratic/incoherent people collapse attachment regardless of other signals. Observer can't form stable mental model; trust can't consolidate.
2. **Dual character:** YES — internal (who you actually are consistently) + displayed (observable across interactions)
3. **Orthogonal to Self-Positioning:** YES — narcissist is Self-Focused but incoherent; consistent mother is not-Self-Focused but coherent
4. **Orthogonal to Emotional Composure:** YES — consistent hothead; calm liar
5. **Not reducible to Self-Positioning species:** YES — Coherence is about consistency of expression, not what species you express
6. **Not reducible to adjacent mechanism:** YES — operates at modulator level, not as external pattern
7. **Observable signals:** YES — longitudinal detection catalog specified
8. **Failure signature:** YES — Model-Collapse is distinct from devaluation (SP) and Contagion-Drain (EC)

All criteria passed. Coherence is a genuine modulator.

### Why Emotional Composure qualifies as a modulator

Passes all 8 criteria:

1. **Multiplicative:** YES — dysregulated person under stress collapses attachment regardless of other signals
2. **Dual character:** YES — internal nervous-system state + displayed composure
3. **Orthogonal to Self-Positioning:** YES — anxious-Self-Focused; calm-Caregiver-Focused
4. **Orthogonal to Coherence:** YES — consistent hothead; calm liar
5. **Not reducible to species:** YES — regulation is about emotional state management, not attention direction
6. **Not reducible to adjacent mechanism:** YES — operates at modulator level
7. **Observable signals:** YES — 5-signal catalog + text + multimodal metrics specified
8. **Failure signature:** YES — Contagion-Drain is distinct

All criteria passed.

### Why Resonance is the 4th attachment variable, not a modulator

Multiple alternatives tested; the decisive test is structural.

**The multiplicative-vs-additive test:** Can Resonance generate attachment WITHOUT any Charm/Hope/Fear present? Multiplicative modulators cannot — a modulator times zero equals zero. If Resonance can generate attachment alone, it operates additively.

**The shared-niche-interest case:** Two strangers meet and discover an unusual shared interest (medieval Ottoman financial instruments). Instant connection. No status games (both non-experts). No benefit-seeking (non-commercial). No fear (strangers). Just mutual recognition of the uncommon interest.

Attachment forms. Charm/Hope/Fear are at or near zero. Therefore Resonance operates additively in f, not multiplicatively as a modulator.

**Further evidence:** Resonance is DYADIC — a property of the pair, not of either individual. Modulators in APT are individual properties. Different architectural category.

### Why Vitality is excluded from the Suite (criterion discipline)

Vitality was a marginal candidate. Close analysis revealed:

- **Normal range:** Vitality behaves additively (slight fatigue → slight signal reduction)
- **Clinical extremes** (dissociation, severe depletion): multiplicative collapse

Modulators in APT are defined as smooth multiplicative gates. Admitting threshold-switching behavior as sufficient would open criterion-loosening — what about clinical depression? Substance intoxication? Severe sleep deprivation? All have threshold effects at extremes.

The smooth-multiplicative criterion is the discipline. Vitality is documented as a degradation factor (real and impactful at extremes) but not a Suite member.

### Why "Reliability" is a theme, not a unified modulator

Considered: collapse Self-Positioning + Coherence + EC into one "Reliability" modulator with three sub-dimensions. Theoretical economy. Rejected because:

**Distinct failure signatures require distinct modulators:**
- SP failure → devaluation (attention seems extractive)
- Coherence failure → Model-Collapse (can't form stable mental model)
- EC failure → Contagion-Drain (emotional load transferred)

Observers experience these differently. "This person seems needy" (SP) vs "This person is erratic" (Coherence) vs "This person is draining" (EC) are different attributions requiring different interventions. Unifying loses diagnostic differentiation.

"Reliability" is preserved as the unifying THEME (all three track aspects of predictability), but the 3 modulators stay distinct.

### Why Coherence has aspects (not species)

Considered: treat Coherence as a genus with species (Moral Coherence, Behavioral Coherence, Narrative Coherence, Role Coherence). Rejected because:

**Species require distinct mechanisms.** Self-Positioning has species because attention has genuinely distinct directional MODES — Self-Focus, Caregiver-Focus, Mission-Focus, etc. — each operating through different attention allocation. Different species, different mechanisms of non-extractive attention.

Coherence's candidate species all produce the same failure signature (Model-Collapse) through the same mechanism (observer can't form stable mental model). They're different CONTENT areas of contradiction, not different mechanisms. This is the structural marker of **aspects**, not species.

Same analysis for Emotional Composure's facets (Physiological + Affective + Recovery).

### Why the non-trigger on Cluster 4 (with honest close-to-line acknowledgment)

Cluster 4's third reopening condition: "multiple future modulators discovered AND all attention-based."

Two new modulators discovered. Question: are they attention-based?

Both have attention-aspects:
- Coherence: attention-consistency over time
- EC: somatic attention (body awareness)

**Dissociation tests** determine whether attention is LOAD-BEARING:

For Coherence: consistent manipulator has attention-consistency (attention stays on scheme) but identity-incoherence (Alignment fails). The modulator fails via Alignment-failure with attention INTACT. Therefore attention is NOT load-bearing; identity/integrity is.

For EC: someone can be attentionally composed (mental calm, no rumination) but physiologically dysregulated (shaking voice). The modulator fails via physiology-failure with attention INTACT. Therefore attention is NOT load-bearing; physiology is.

**Conclusion:** the load-bearing aspects are non-attention. Cluster 4 condition 3 is not satisfied. Non-trigger defensible.

**Honest acknowledgment:** the attention-aspects ARE present. The dissociation tests defend the non-trigger, but the distinction is subtle. Future empirical operationalization might show attention-aspects are actually more load-bearing than the dissociation tests suggested. Flag for future re-examination.

This is the D5 refinement from critique — explicit close-to-line note required in spec.

### Why orthogonality is theoretical, not empirical

Claim: the 3 modulators are orthogonal dimensions.

Orthogonality has two meanings:
- **Theoretical:** dissociation possible at definition level
- **Empirical:** independent in practice (no correlation)

The claim made is THEORETICAL. Dissociation tests (consistent hothead, calm liar, narcissist, etc.) show that each pair of modulators can independently vary at the definition level — there exist logically-possible cases where one is high and the other low.

Empirical independence is NOT claimed. Real-world correlation is expected. Consistent people tend to be regulated. Self-Focused people tend to be coherent. These correlations don't collapse the theoretical orthogonality — they reflect shared developmental paths, not mechanism-identity.

This is the D8 refinement from critique — explicit epistemic-precision statement required.
