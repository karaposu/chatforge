## Phase 2 — Adversarial Evaluation

### Candidate ALPHA — Full iteration-3.1 with outcome-independence as narrative spine

**Bundle:** All 6 refinements per innovation's proposal + outcome-independence elevated to organizing narrative spine + tiered audit (1Q gateway + 7P full) + separate iteration-3.1 finding + practice protocols referenced + multimodal metrics in body + short descriptive oscillation paragraph + 1-paragraph character scoping.

**Preview:** High on D1, D3, D4, D5. Some concerns on D6 (parsimony across 6 additions + narrative-spine elevation), D7 (innovation goes beyond sensemaking in elevating outcome-independence), and D9 (parse ambiguity in independence clause — flagged below).

**Prosecution:**

1. **Outcome-independence elevation exceeds sensemaking's commitment.** Sensemaking stabilized outcome-independence as ONE of seven audit checks (audit item 4). Innovation elevates it to NARRATIVE SPINE — the concept that organizes the entire iteration-3.1. This is a material move beyond sensemaking. If innovation can do this, what stops future innovations from unilaterally elevating any of sensemaking's checks to organizing status? Scope discipline concern.

2. **Independence clause has parse ambiguity.** The proposed wording is "attention on own priorities independent of the counterparty's response to resolve them." The "to resolve them" clause dangles: is it (a) a purpose clause modifying "counterparty's response" (the counterparty's response is for-the-purpose-of resolving them — i.e., neediness) or (b) a relative clause modifying "priorities" (priorities that would-otherwise-need-counterparty's-response to resolve)? The intended meaning is clear to someone who already understands the concept, but a first-time reader may parse wrongly. D9 concern.

3. **Multimodal metrics expand iteration-3.1 scope beyond operational dynamics.** Multimodal metrics are a catalog EXTENSION (new channel), not a dynamics REFINEMENT. Decomposition argued they belong with temporal precedence because body-language IS what enables g's instantaneous display. But critique should test: could iteration-3.1 be delivered without multimodal, deferring to iteration-3.3?

4. **Practice protocols (Stoic, performance-psychology, Rogers, Buddhist) blur APT's descriptive scope.** APT is a descriptive theory of attention mechanics. Importing Stoic practice + Rogers' UPR + etc. introduces prescriptive content that previously was scoped out. The "prescriptive-guidance layer" framing from iteration-2 is being re-activated and extended — is this appropriate?

5. **Tiered audit may introduce premature-stopping risk.** Users with gateway-YES may not engage the deeper 7-point check when they should. 1-question audits are prone to self-deception (the user reports "I'd be okay if they don't respond" while actually experiencing collapse underneath).

**Defense:**

1. **D1 wins decisively.** All 5 sub-questions are concretely addressed: Q1 (character) via scoping paragraph; Q2 (approach) via temporal precedence + multimodal; Q3 (neediness) via independence clause — load-bearing fix; Q4 (attention-giving) via Caregiver-Focus + context-fit + oscillation; Q5 (calibration) via tiered audit. No sub-question left unresolved.

2. **Outcome-independence elevation is justified structurally, not unilaterally.** Innovation's Cluster A converged from THREE mechanisms (Combination Focused + Inversion Focused + Domain Transfer Focused/Stoic). Three-mechanism convergence IS the signal that justifies elevation. Sensemaking didn't SEE the narrative-spine potential because that's not sensemaking's job — sensemaking stabilized the structural claim; innovation surfaced the organizing framing. Both disciplines did their work. Critique's question isn't "did innovation overreach?" but "is the elevation evidence-supported?" — answer: yes.

3. **No new concepts introduced.** Innovation's 4.C (Constraint Manipulation Contrarian) explicitly tested and confirmed that iteration-3.1 introduces no new concepts — every refinement is a recombination or explication of iteration-3 concepts. This is strong architectural validation. Parsimony concern (D6) is less weighty than it appears — the concept set doesn't grow; the structural additions are all reuses.

4. **Multimodal metrics belong with temporal precedence per decomposition.** Decomposition grouped them together because the causal link (multimodal signals ARE what enable g's instantaneous display-bandwidth) is tight. Separating them weakens the approach-dynamics story. Keeping them together serves D1 (approach sub-question resolution) and D3 (operational testability in approach contexts).

5. **Practice protocols are REFERENCED, not imported.** Innovation's framing is "one-line references with pointers to mature traditions" — not deep theoretical integration. Stoic practice is noted as COMPATIBLE with Self-Focus cultivation; APT doesn't adopt Stoic metaphysics. The prescriptive-guidance layer was established in iteration-2; iteration-3.1 extends it with references, not new content.

**Collision:**

- Prosecution's #1 (outcome-independence elevation concern) is defused by Defense's #2 — three-mechanism convergence is the standard signal for elevation. Innovation is supposed to surface framings sensemaking missed; that's the loop's design.
- Prosecution's #2 (parse ambiguity in independence clause) is REAL and unresolved. Defense doesn't address it directly. **Critique should require clarification.**
- Prosecution's #3 (multimodal scope) is defused by decomposition's coupling analysis — multimodal is tightly coupled with temporal precedence; separating loses causal connection.
- Prosecution's #4 (practice protocols blur scope) is defused by Defense's #5 — references, not imports.
- Prosecution's #5 (tiered audit premature-stopping) is a real user-risk. Mitigation: explicit guidance that gateway-YES doesn't preclude deeper audit if outcomes surprise.

**Position:**

#### 1. Definition Tightening (P1) — load-bearing, with wording refinement

**Proposed refined wording:** ~~"attention on own priorities independent of the counterparty's response to resolve them"~~

→ **"attention on own priorities whose resolution doesn't depend on the counterparty's response"**

(OR equivalently: "attention on own priorities — where resolution doesn't require the counterparty's response")

The refined wording removes the parse ambiguity of the original. "Whose resolution doesn't depend on the counterparty's response" is unambiguous — the subject is the priorities, the predicate is resolution-independence.

Applied across species:
- **Self-Focus** = attention on own priorities whose resolution doesn't depend on counterparty's response
- **Caregiver-Focus** = non-extractive attention on counterparty's wellbeing, where the caring doesn't depend on their approval
- **Mission-Focus** = attention on a larger cause whose pursuit doesn't depend on current counterparty's response
- **Task-Focus, Dialogue-Focus, Transcendent-Focus** = analogous patterns
- **Supplication (negative pole)** = attention whose resolution IS dependent on counterparty's response
