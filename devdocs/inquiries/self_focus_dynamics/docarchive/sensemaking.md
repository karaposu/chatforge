## Phase 3 — Ambiguity Collapse

### Ambiguity 1 — Independence clause: definition CHANGE or CLARIFICATION?

**Resolution:** CLARIFICATION. The genus's non-extractivity was always intended to inherit into species. iteration-3 left the inheritance implicit. iteration-3.1 makes it explicit.

**Counter-interpretation test:** Could be read as definition change if readers interpret the species definition WITHOUT the genus context. This happens when people encounter "Self-Focus = attention on own priorities" in isolation and apply it literally.

**Why the counter fails structurally:** Genus-species hierarchies always imply species inherit genus properties unless explicitly overridden. iteration-3 doesn't override; it just doesn't re-state. Exploration's Q3 probe showed this gap caused real misread. Closing the gap isn't changing theory — it's making theory inheritance visible.

**What's now fixed:** Species-level definitions in iteration-3.1 all explicitly include the independence-of-counterparty-response clause (or equivalent non-extractivity inheritance). For Self-Focus: "attention on own priorities independent of counterparty's response to resolve them."

**What's no longer allowed:** Reading "attention on own priorities" without the independence clause (confusion gap closed).

**Depends on:** Neediness-productive distinction; user's calibration audit (outcome-independence check).

**What changed in the model:** Species definitions become expositionally self-sufficient; genus inheritance made visible.

### Ambiguity 2 — Temporal precedence: new mechanism or detail of existing?

**Resolution:** DETAIL. The multiplicative gating formula is unchanged. What's added is descriptive of TIME-PROFILE: g has instantaneous display-bandwidth (posture, voice, premise, eye contact all readable in seconds); f has build-over-time bandwidth (Charm, Hope, Fear need content development).

**Counter test:** Could "first-contact modulator" be a separate mechanism?

**Why counter fails:** A separate mechanism would require a different math structure. The gating formula works at every moment. What differs over time is SIGNAL DENSITY RATIO — not the formula. No new mechanism needed; describing the mechanism's temporal profile suffices.

**What's now fixed:** Temporal precedence as descriptive addendum to the mechanism section of iteration-3 finding. Specific wording: "g operates at instantaneous bandwidth via observable display signals (posture, voice, premise, eye contact, response calibration, exit-willingness); f develops over longer time-horizons. In short windows (first 5-30 seconds of first-contact), the g signal dominates the attachment-probability because f hasn't fully developed yet. Multiplicative gating still holds at every moment; the temporal signal-density ratio is what shifts over time."

**What's no longer allowed:** Treating "first-contact modulator" as a separate mechanism from the multiplicative gating.

**Depends on:** Approach-specific diagnostic wording; multimodal metrics catalog extension (body-language signals are g-bandwidth in approach contexts).

**What changed in the model:** The mechanism gets a richer temporal description, preserving its formal structure.

### Ambiguity 3 — Species-context-fit: prescriptive or descriptive?

**Resolution:** PRESCRIPTIVE. Guidance about which species to activate for intended outcomes. Not an empirical claim about which species emerges in which context.

**Counter test:** Is it actually descriptive — empirically, mothers operate in Caregiver-Focus, CEOs operate in Mission-Focus?

**Why partially both, resolved as prescriptive:** Descriptive regularities exist (mothers tend toward Caregiver-Focus; elite performers tend toward Task-Focus), but the SPEC-RELEVANT claim is prescriptive: IF you want healthy attachment in context X, THEN use species Y. The descriptive regularity supports the prescriptive claim but they're different types of statements.

**What's now fixed:** Species-context-fit belongs in the iteration-3.1 prescriptive-guidance layer (separate from descriptive mechanism). Framework:

- Value-establishing contexts → Self-Focus primary
- Established trusting relationships → Caregiver-Focus primary with Self-Focus oscillation
- Hierarchical / professional → Task-Focus or Self-Focus depending on role
- Caring / parenting / mentoring → Caregiver-Focus primary
- Performance / craft → Task-Focus primary
- Cause-driven leadership → Mission-Focus primary

**What's no longer allowed:** Treating species-context-fit as a descriptive-theory claim without the prescriptive framing.

**Depends on:** iteration-3.1 prescriptive-guidance section; user-facing calibration audit (context-fit check).

**What changed in the model:** Explicit prescriptive layer separate from descriptive mechanism.

### Ambiguity 6 — 7-point calibration audit: weighted-score, ordered-sequence, or independent-composite?
