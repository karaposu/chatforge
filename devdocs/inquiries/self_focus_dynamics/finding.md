# Finding: self_focus_dynamics

**This finding is iteration-3.1 of the APT Self-Positioning theory — an additive dynamic-layer refinement of iteration-3 (`self_focus_generalization/finding.md`).** It supersedes iteration-3 at the vocabulary-tightening, audit-expansion, and prescriptive-guidance-extension levels. It inherits iteration-3's architectural commitments unchanged: genus/species taxonomy (Non-Extractive Attention genus; Self-Focus + Caregiver-Focus primary species; Mission-Focus / Task-Focus / Dialogue-Focus / Transcendent-Focus secondary species; Supplication as negative pole); multiplicative gating mechanism; trait-with-bearings; 5-signal catalog and 6 text-feature operational metrics; 2×2 diagnostic structure; devaluation failure signature; two-level naming (theoretical Non-Extractive Attention + user-facing Self-Focus for LinkedIn); Cluster 4 elevated status (iteration-4 substrate reframe planned for future, not triggered).

**The central insight of iteration-3.1 is outcome-independence** — the property of attention whose resolution doesn't depend on the counterparty's response. This is what distinguishes productive non-extractive modes (all species) from needy self-attention and Supplication. The iteration-3.1 refinement makes outcome-independence explicit in species definitions, positions it as the load-bearing calibration check, and provides structural support across 6 additive components.

iteration-3.1 introduces **no new concepts**. Every refinement is a tightening, restating, or recombination of iteration-3 vocabulary.

---

## Question

The prior inquiry (`self_focus_generalization`) established the APT Self-Positioning theory at iteration-3: Non-Extractive Attention genus with two primary species (Self-Focus, Caregiver-Focus) and four secondary species, with a multiplicative gating mechanism, 5-signal catalog, and 2×2 diagnostic preserved from earlier iterations.

The user then raised five related sub-questions about how this theory *operates* in practice, at the moment-to-moment interaction level:

1. **Character:** Self-Focus leaves an observable mark on others; people remember each other through what might be called "Self-Focus shape." Is this what we usually call character, and does this belong in APT's scope?

2. **Approach dynamics:** In short first-contact interactions (cold approach, pitch, introduction — roughly 5 minutes), there isn't enough time for Charm/Hope/Fear signals to fully develop. Yet outcomes diverge sharply: two men can approach the same woman with similar looks and credentials; one gets reciprocation, the other dismissal. The theory predicts this turns on how Self-Focus is conveyed in the moment. Is the 5-minute approach a special case, or does it require its own sub-mechanism?

3. **Neediness paradox:** A needy person is also attending to themselves (their own lack, wanting). But neediness is unattractive — it fails the modulator the same way Supplication does. Why? What distinguishes **productive Self-Focus** (attention on own agenda/craft/desire — amplifies attachment) from **needy self-attention** (attention on own lack/wanting — collapses attachment)? Both are self-directed.

4. **Attention-giving paradox:** In established relationships (partner, boss, close friend, child), giving attention to the specific other is both expected and healthy. Yet the theory says non-Self-Focus collapses the modulator. How do these coexist? Is there a dynamic where diminishing Self-Focus constitutes attention-giving, and does that only work when Self-Focus was established in the first place?

5. **Calibration check:** How does one know what is the *right amount* of Self-Focus for a given context? By what observable or felt signal can calibration be checked?

**The inquiry question:** What are the operational dynamics of Self-Focus at the moment-to-moment interaction level — answering all five sub-questions coherently, clarifying productive vs needy self-attention, operationalizing the approach case, integrating character-perception with the existing theory, providing calibration heuristics, and resolving the attention-giving paradox?

**The goal:** A coherent framework strong enough to either add a dedicated "Operational Dynamics" section to the iteration-3 finding, or warrant an iteration-3.1 refinement.

## Finding

### The central insight: outcome-independence as the organizing concept

**Iteration-3.1's central theoretical move is to make outcome-independence explicit and load-bearing.** Outcome-independence is the property of attention whose resolution doesn't depend on the counterparty's response. This property was always implicit in iteration-3's genus definition (Non-Extractive Attention means attention not on harvesting the counterparty's response); iteration-3.1 makes it explicit at species level and foregrounds it as the decisive calibration check.

This one property is what distinguishes:
- **Productive non-extractive attention (all species)** — your state's resolution doesn't depend on their response
- **Needy self-attention** — your state's resolution depends on their response, even though your attention is self-directed in content
- **Supplication / Extractive Attention** — your state's resolution depends on their response, with attention explicitly on securing it

Outcome-independence is what the user cultivates; outcome-dependence is what collapses the modulator. Whether the attention is on self (Self-Focus) or on the other (Caregiver-Focus) or on a cause (Mission-Focus) or on a craft (Task-Focus) is secondary — what matters first is whether the attention is non-extractive. Outcome-independence is the marker of non-extractive-ness made operational.

### The six refinement components of iteration-3.1

#### 1. Species-definition tightening (the load-bearing fix)

**The refined definition for Self-Focus species:**

> **Self-Focus = attention on own priorities whose resolution doesn't depend on the counterparty's response.**

The key clause — *whose resolution doesn't depend on the counterparty's response* — makes the genus's non-extractivity explicit at species level. This closes a quiet self-contradiction in iteration-3: the genus was defined by non-extractivity, but species definitions didn't carry the non-extractivity forward in their surface wording, allowing needy self-attention to misread as Self-Focus.

Applied across species:

- **Self-Focus species** = attention on own priorities whose resolution doesn't depend on the counterparty's response
- **Caregiver-Focus species** = non-extractive attention on the counterparty's wellbeing, where the caring doesn't depend on their approval
- **Mission-Focus species** = attention on a larger cause whose pursuit doesn't depend on the current counterparty's response
- **Task-Focus species** = attention on the craft / the work itself, whose value doesn't depend on audience response
- **Dialogue-Focus species** = attention on a shared inquiry whose progression doesn't depend on external validation
- **Transcendent-Focus species** = attention on an ideal / abstract whose reality doesn't depend on counterparty endorsement
- **Supplication (negative pole)** = attention whose resolution IS dependent on the counterparty's response

Genus-level definition unchanged: Non-Extractive Attention remains defined by non-harvesting of the counterparty's response, non-validation, non-approval-extraction.

**Contrast examples** that make the distinction crystal clear:

| Mode | Content of attention | Structural property |
|---|---|---|
| Productive Self-Focus | "I'm working on X; here's what I think about it" | Self-sufficient; continues whether they engage or not |
| Needy self-attention | "I'm feeling lonely / worried / inadequate; tell me I'm okay" | Self-directed but other-dependent for resolution |
| Productive Caregiver-Focus | "I care about your wellbeing" | Caring happens without needing approval for caring |
| Approval-harvesting-as-care | "I'm attending to you so you'll like me" | Attention is instrumental to approval-extraction |

Both Productive modes are outcome-independent. Both failure modes are outcome-dependent despite different surface content.

#### 2. Mechanism temporal detail + multimodal metrics

The multiplicative gating formula remains unchanged:

```
Attachment ≈ f(charm, hope, fear) × g(non-extractive attention)
```

**What iteration-3.1 adds:** g operates at **instantaneous display-bandwidth** (readable within seconds), while f develops over longer time-horizons. In short windows (first 5-30 seconds of first-contact), the g signal dominates the attachment-probability because f hasn't fully developed yet.

This explains the approach paradox directly: in a 5-minute approach, two men with similar Charm depth / Hope-inducing credentials / Fear-effective status get different outcomes because their Self-Focus display (g-signal) dominates. Charm needs more time to unfold; Self-Focus reads in the first seconds from posture, voice, opening premise, eye contact, response calibration, and exit readiness.

**Same formula. Different temporal signal-density ratio.** g dominates early; f catches up later.

**Multimodal metrics catalog extension.** The iteration-3 operational catalog had 6 text-feature metrics (opening verb class, pronoun ratio, question-to-statement ratio, self-justification clause count, elaboration gradient, closing assumption tone). Iteration-3.1 adds 6 parallel multimodal metrics for face-to-face and embodied contexts:

| Multimodal metric | What it measures | High g signal | Low g signal |
|---|---|---|---|
| Posture | Body-language grounding | Relaxed, upright, own-centered | Fidgety, closed, leaning-toward |
| Voice cadence / pitch | Speech-pattern self-regulation | Calm, unrushed, lower-pitched | Rushed, pitched-up, apologetic |
| Eye contact pattern | Gaze-regulation | Steady, unhurried, direct | Darting, avoiding, over-eager |
| Body alignment | Spatial orientation | Oriented at from confident stance | Looming-toward, pursuing-with-body |
| Response-timing tempo | Reactivity tempo | Unhurried response; comfortable pauses | Immediate-reactive; no silence tolerance |
| Exit readiness | Physical comfort with ending | Comfortable with natural close | Clingy, extending, reluctant |

Together, 12 metrics (6 text-feature + 6 multimodal) operationalize Displayed Non-Extractive Attention across text, face-to-face, and mixed channels. Deeper multimodal specification (detection rules, cross-modal weighting, edge cases) is flagged for iteration-3.3 (multimodal-extension spec).

#### 3. Prescriptive dynamics: species-context-fit + oscillation

**Species-context-fit framework** (prescriptive guidance — which species to cultivate for intended outcomes in each context):

| Context | Primary species | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Value-establishing (approach, cold outreach, sales first-contact) | **Self-Focus** | User's LinkedIn case |
| Established trusting relationships (long-term partners, close friends) | **Caregiver-Focus** with Self-Focus oscillation | Healthy rhythm |
| Hierarchical / professional (employee-boss, team member-leader) | **Task-Focus** or **Self-Focus** depending on role | Context-role dependency |
| Caring / parenting / mentoring | **Caregiver-Focus** | Non-extractive attention on growth |
| Performance / craft | **Task-Focus** | Performance-psychology pedigree |
| Cause-driven leadership | **Mission-Focus** | Visionary-leader archetype |
| Philosophical / intellectual dialogue | **Dialogue-Focus** | Shared-inquiry mode |
| Contemplative / spiritual | **Transcendent-Focus** | Rare in professional contexts |

Species-context-fit is **prescriptive** — it's guidance about what to cultivate for intended outcomes, not a descriptive claim about which species empirically appears in each context. The descriptive-mechanism layer (multiplicative gating, genus, species definitions) is unchanged; prescriptive guidance is a separate layer that applies the descriptive mechanism to practical choice.

**Oscillation dynamics.** Healthy interactions oscillate between species over time. In established relationships, sustained pure Self-Focus reads as cold / indifferent; sustained pure Caregiver-Focus reads as dependent / sycophantic. Healthy rhythm alternates: presenting your own experience, listening attentively, asserting from your ground, receiving their response. Sustained imbalance in either direction signals pathology. Formal modeling of oscillation dynamics (rhythm parameters, optimal frequencies) is deferred to future work.

**Practice protocol references** (for cultivating outcome-independence and species-specific skills):

- **Stoic dichotomy of control** (Epictetus; Marcus Aurelius) — mature practice tradition directly aligned with outcome-independence. Daily Stoic journaling and evening-review practices cultivate distinguishing what's up to you (your attention, actions, responses) from what isn't (others' responses, outcomes) — which IS outcome-independence in daily discipline.
- **Performance-psychology task-focus** — pre-performance routines, process cues, body-scan grounding are directly importable as Self-Focus cultivation tools.
- **Rogers' Unconditional Positive Regard** — operationalizes Caregiver-Focus via three conditions: congruence (therapist is genuine, not performing), unconditional positive regard (acceptance without extraction), empathic understanding (attention truly on the counterparty's world).
- **Buddhist non-grasping attention / Zen beginner's mind** — parallel concepts that may inform cultivation practices (one-line mention only; APT doesn't adopt Buddhist metaphysics).

These are **references**, not framework imports. APT stays descriptive with a prescriptive-guidance layer that points to mature traditions for practice.

#### 4. Character-perception scoping

**Across repeated interactions, an individual's stable tendencies in non-extractive-attention expression — their species bearing (Self-Focus-dominant, Caregiver-Focus-dominant, etc.) plus the level they operate at — become part of what observers recognize as the person's character.** APT's trait-with-bearings structure captures this Self-Positioning dimension of character perception.

**Character in full is broader than APT's scope.** Character includes values, moral disposition, cognitive style, and specific choices. Self-Positioning is particularly visible because every interaction exhibits it (while other character dimensions emerge only under specific conditions), which disproportionately weights it in character-perception — but APT doesn't claim to cover character broadly. APT Profiling outputs capture the Self-Positioning component; the rest is outside APT's scope.

#### 5. Tiered calibration audit

Iteration-2 established a 5-question audit for user-facing LinkedIn diagnostic. Iteration-3.1 **supplements** this with a tiered calibration audit for finer diagnosis across contexts.

**Gateway 1-question audit:**

> **"Am I okay if they don't respond favorably?"**
>
> If a genuine YES: your non-extractive attention is likely well-calibrated. Continue.
>
> If NO or unsure (any form of collapse, anxiety, or spiral under the prospect of non-response): use the full 7-point audit to diagnose the specific miscalibration.
>
> *Important:* gateway-YES is provisional. If outcomes surprise you (unexpected dismissal, confusion about what went wrong), run the full 7-point audit to check whether the gateway self-report was accurate — self-deception in the 1-question form is a real risk.

**Full 7-point audit** (independent-composite — each check addresses a distinct miscalibration dimension):

1. **Context check** — What context am I in? (value-establishing / established / hierarchical / caring / performance / cause-driven / dialogue / contemplative)
2. **Species check** — Which non-extractive-attention species is active for me?
3. **Species-context fit** — Is this species appropriate for this context? (See species-context-fit table.)
4. **Outcome-independence** — Am I okay if they don't respond favorably? *(the decisive test; distinguishes productive from needy)*
5. **Internal state** — Was I grounded or anxious during the exchange?
6. **Counterparty-response curve** — Was their attention rising or falling through the exchange?
7. **Exchange quality** — Mutual benefit or extraction / drain?

Interpretation: independent-composite. Each check catches a distinct miscalibration dimension. If all pass: high confidence. If any fails: that check identifies the specific miscalibration.

**Relationship to iteration-2's 5-question audit:** the 5-question audit remains the quick LinkedIn diagnostic for value-establishing contexts (professional outreach, cold contact). The tiered 7-point audit is for finer calibration across diverse contexts.

**Forward-looking value.** As the user applies the audit across 20-50-100 interactions, personal subtype patterns emerge:
- Personal species tendency becomes clear (e.g., "I default to Self-Focus in professional contexts; Caregiver-Focus with close friends; Mission-Focus for projects I care about")
- Personal failure-mode sub-type emerges (e.g., "my Try-Hard pattern is predominantly self-justification-dominant with over-elaboration")
- Specific miscalibration triggers surface (e.g., "I collapse to Supplication when the counterparty has high formal status")

The audit becomes a pattern-surfacing tool, not just a per-interaction diagnostic.

#### 6. Architectural inheritance (unchanged from iteration-3)

- **Path α:** Self-Positioning remains a named modulator between Domain 1 (Attachment) and Domain 2 (Presentation) in APT.
- **Multiplicative gating:** `Attachment ≈ f(charm, hope, fear) × g(non-extractive attention)` — formula unchanged. Temporal precedence is a description of how the formula operates over time, not a change to the formula.
- **Trait-with-bearings:** individuals have stable central tendencies in which species they operate in and what level they operate at. Bearings now include outcome-independence as a trait dimension (some people have naturally high outcome-independence; others have to cultivate it).
- **5-signal catalog:** Withholding-Signal, Premise-Posture, Self-Justification-Density, Exit-Willingness, Rhythm-Comfort. Unchanged. Operationalized by 6 text-feature metrics + 6 multimodal metrics = 12 metrics across channels.
- **2×2 diagnostic:** structure unchanged. Vertical axis: Self-Focus level (for user LinkedIn application); theoretical level: Non-Extractive Attention. Horizontal axis: Investment-Asymmetry. Four quadrants: Confident Selector / Respected Expert / Disengaged / Try-Hard.
- **Devaluation failure signature:** when `g` collapses, high `f(charm, hope, fear)` produces near-zero real attachment. The other party registers asymmetric-need and discounts signals post-hoc ("this guy doesn't deserve this"). Unchanged.
- **Two-level naming:** theoretical-level = Non-Extractive Attention (genus); user-facing level = Self-Focus (primary species for value-establishing contexts).
- **Cluster 4 status:** ELEVATED (from iteration-3) — iteration-4 substrate reframe inquiry should be planned for future, not triggered in iteration-3.1. Reopening conditions preserved unchanged.

### What iteration-3.1 resolves — the five sub-questions

**Q1 (Character):** APT's trait-with-bearings captures the Self-Positioning dimension of character perception (the "visible in every interaction" aspect). Character in full includes values, moral disposition, cognitive style, specific choices. APT contributes to character-perception; it doesn't equal character theory. Acknowledgment paragraph in iteration-3.1; no architectural change.

**Q2 (Approach):** Self-Focus has temporal precedence in short windows because g operates at instantaneous display-bandwidth while Charm/Hope/Fear need more time to build. In 5-minute approaches, the approach's outcome is largely determined by Self-Focus display (posture, voice, opening premise, eye contact, response calibration, exit readiness). Same multiplicative gating formula; different temporal signal-density ratio. Multimodal metrics catalog extends the operational toolkit for approach contexts.

**Q3 (Neediness):** Neediness is Extractive self-attention — attention on own lack whose resolution depends on the counterparty's response. Surface self-directed; structurally other-dependent. Fits the Supplication pole mechanically. The refined species definition — "attention on own priorities whose resolution doesn't depend on the counterparty's response" — makes this distinction explicit. Both productive Self-Focus and neediness attend to self; only productive Self-Focus is outcome-independent.

**Q4 (Attention-giving paradox):** Resolved via Caregiver-Focus species (already in iteration-3) + species-context-fit framework + oscillation dynamics. Legitimate attention-giving in established relationships is Caregiver-Focus (non-extractive attention on the other). Context determines which species is primary; healthy interactions oscillate. Failing attention-giving in value-establishing contexts (the man-approaching-woman giving-all-attention case) is Supplication dressed as Caregiver-Focus — same genus-test as neediness.

**Q5 (Calibration):** Tiered 7-point audit with outcome-independence (gateway question + audit item 4) as the decisive test. Different checks catch different miscalibration types. Gateway enables quick self-check; full audit for finer diagnosis. Practice protocols (Stoic / performance-psychology / Rogers) available for cultivation.

Self-Focus species remains the user-facing primary term for value-establishing contexts. The refined wording ("attention on own priorities whose resolution doesn't depend on the counterparty's response") shows up in the definition section but doesn't change the diagnostic workflow.

## Reasoning

### Why iteration-3.1 rather than an addendum

The refinement could have been a short addendum to iteration-3's finding, but critique adjudicated against that (Candidate GAMMA — minimalist). The minimalist path fails D1 — it addresses only 1 of the 5 sub-questions (neediness) without resolving the other 4. The user's inquiry had 5 sub-questions; serving only 1 is sub-goal.

**Full iteration-3.1 (Candidate ALPHA) with outcome-independence as narrative spine** emerged as the winning proposal. Innovation's 3-mechanism convergence (Combination Focused + Inversion Focused + Domain Transfer Focused via Stoicism) provided structural justification for elevating outcome-independence from sensemaking's "one check among seven" to iteration-3.1's organizing concept. Critique stress-tested this elevation via Candidate BETA (no spine) and found BETA hits boundary failures on D1 (pedagogical resolution of sub-questions), D5 (definitional sharpness of productive-vs-needy), and D9 (pedagogical clarity). The elevation is evidence-supported.

### Why the wording refinement

Innovation proposed "attention on own priorities **independent of** the counterparty's response to resolve them." Critique identified a parse ambiguity: the "to resolve them" clause dangles — is it (a) a purpose clause modifying the counterparty's response, or (b) a relative clause modifying the priorities? The intended meaning is clear to someone who already understands the concept, but first-time readers may parse wrongly.

**Refined wording:** "attention on own priorities **whose resolution doesn't depend on** the counterparty's response" — direct subject-predicate structure, no parse ambiguity.

The refined wording is equivalent in meaning to the original but more pedagogically clear. Pedagogical clarity (D9) matters specifically for the load-bearing independence clause, since the load-bearing fix depends on the distinction landing for the reader.

### Why "no new concepts" discipline

Innovation's Constraint Manipulation Contrarian (4.C) explicitly tested whether iteration-3.1 introduces any new concepts. Result: every refinement is a tightening, restating, or recombination of iteration-3 concepts. The independence clause restates genus non-extractivity at species level. Temporal precedence describes g's display-bandwidth property of the existing mechanism. Species-context-fit recombines existing species with existing contexts. Oscillation restates trait-with-bearings dynamics over time. Character-scoping restates trait-with-bearings scope relative to character perception. Multimodal metrics restate the 5-signal catalog in different channels. 7-point audit restates existing checks with new independent-composite structure.

Explicitly stating "no new concepts" in the introduction validates the additive framing and prevents scope creep in future iterations.

### Why tiered audit over single-version alternatives

Multiple audit configurations were evaluated. The tiered structure (gateway 1-question + full 7-point composite) serves activation-energy (gateway is low-barrier), depth-of-application (full is detailed), and user-range (novice to sophisticated). Critique adjudicated:

- **DELTA-3 (single-indicator only)** — KILLED on D1. 1-question audit alone doesn't resolve the calibration sub-question with sufficient detail across contexts; the user needs more than one check to catch distinct miscalibration types.
- **DELTA-2 (7-point only, no gateway)** — REFINED to DELTA-1. Activation energy is too high without a gateway; users may not audit at all if it requires 7 questions every time.
- **DELTA-4 (full + operationalization tools)** — REFINED to DELTA-1. Operationalization tools (self-observation protocols, counterparty-response tracking, time-lag guidance) are downstream work; iteration-3.1 scope should not expand to include them.
- **DELTA-1 (gateway + full composite)** — SURVIVED as winner. Mitigation for premature-stopping risk: explicit guidance that gateway-YES is provisional; if outcomes surprise, run the full audit.

### Why practice protocols as references, not imports

Critique adjudicated between full integration (ZETA-2), reference-only (ZETA-1), and omission (ZETA-3):

- **ZETA-2 (full integration)** — KILLED on scope violation. Importing Stoic metaphysics / Rogers' humanistic-psychology framework / Buddhist theology into APT would make APT more prescriptive and bring theological/cultural baggage. Different Buddhist schools disagree; different Stoic interpretations conflict; importing locks APT to a specific version.
- **ZETA-3 (no references)** — REFINED to ZETA-1. Omission misses the value of mature practice traditions. Users who want to cultivate outcome-independence benefit from knowing Stoic daily journaling exists.
- **ZETA-1 (references only)** — SURVIVED. One-line references with pointers preserve APT's descriptive scope while enabling users to access mature pedagogies outside APT.

### Why multimodal metrics in iteration-3.1 body (not deferred to iteration-3.3)

Decomposition identified tight coupling between temporal precedence and multimodal metrics: body-language signals ARE what enable g's instantaneous display-bandwidth in face-to-face contexts. Separating them (deferring multimodal to iteration-3.3) would weaken the approach-dynamics story.

Critique adjudicated:
- **ETA-2 (defer multimodal to 3.3)** — REFINED to ETA-1. Loses causal connection; Q2 approach-dynamics sub-question not cleanly resolved.
- **ETA-1 (multimodal in iteration-3.1 body)** — SURVIVED. Short parallel subsection (6 multimodal metrics mirroring 6 text-feature metrics) serves the coupling without expanding scope unduly. Deeper multimodal specification (detection rules, cross-modal weighting, edge cases) deferred to iteration-3.3.

### Why separate iteration-3.1 finding over in-place update

Critique adjudicated:
- **EPSILON-2 (in-place update of iteration-3 finding)** — REFINED to EPSILON-1. Loses version history; iteration-3 as stable state disappears; silent updates confuse cross-references from other inquiries.
- **EPSILON-1 (separate iteration-3.1 finding)** — SURVIVED. MVL+ convention; preserves version history; clear "what changed when" audit trail; user can post-hoc update iteration-3 in place if preferred.

### Other rejected approaches (briefly)

- **No-spine framing (BETA)** refined to ALPHA because sensemaking's "outcome-independence as one check" is pedagogically thinner than innovation's "outcome-independence as spine"; the spine gives the refinement coherence.
- **Iteration-4 substrate reframe triggering** — out of scope for this inquiry. Cluster 4 status unchanged from iteration-3 (elevated, planned for future).
- **Full character theory expansion** — out of scope; would exceed APT's descriptive domain.
- **Formal oscillation model** — no data to formalize; pattern-level description suffices for iteration-3.1.

### Contradictions reconciled across disciplines

**Exploration surfaced 6 refinement candidates; sensemaking located them in iteration-3's structure; innovation elevated outcome-independence to narrative spine (going beyond sensemaking); critique stress-tested the elevation and confirmed it.** This is the loop working as designed — exploration maps, sensemaking stabilizes, innovation surfaces framings sensemaking missed, critique adjudicates whether the framings survive.

**Decomposition's P3 bundling (case reconciliation + pro-social refinement as one piece) was inherited from iteration-3's decomposition; iteration-3.1's decomposition bundled differently (species-definition tightening as foundational P1; other pieces following).** Each inquiry's decomposition is scoped to its own question; no inter-inquiry decomposition inconsistency.

**Critique's parse-ambiguity finding on the independence clause is the material new refinement beyond innovation's proposal.** Innovation proposed wording; critique applied D9 pedagogical clarity; the ambiguity was caught. This is D9's payoff — a dimension added specifically for this iteration because the neediness gap was partly pedagogical.
